20. Contoh prohibition di rumah


Nominal prohibition yang lebih sopan dengan ditambah “Please”

Please do not be crazy!
(tolong jangan gila!)

Pease don’t be disappointed!
(tolong jangan kecewa!)

Please don’t be hopeless!
(Tolong jangan putus asa!)

Kalimat larangan dengan pola No + Ving

No smoking!
(Jangan merokok!)

No drinking!
(Jangan minum!)

No parking here!
(Jangan parkir di sini!)

No eating in the class!
(Jangan makan di kelas!)

No copying your friend’s work!
(Jangan mencontek pekerjaan temanmu!)

Contoh kalimat prohibition dengan pola yang lain.

You are not allowed to come late!
(Kamu tidak diperbolehkan datang terlambat!)

You cannot follow the test!
(Kamu tidak bisa mengikuti tes!)

He is not permitted to bring food from outside!
(Dia tidak diijinkan membawa makanan dari luar!)

You mustn’t give your book to her!
(Kamu tidak harus memberi bukumu kepadanya! )

Verbal prohibition yang lebih sopan ditambah dengan kata “please”

Please do not go!
(Tolong jangan pergi!)

Please do not leave me alone!
(Mohon jangan tinggalkan aku sendiri!)

Please do not make any noise!
(Mohon jangan rebut!)

Please don’t cry!
(Tolong jangan menagis!)

Please don’t drive carelessly!
(Tolong jangan mengebut!)

Please don’t talk too much!
(Tolong jangan berbicara terlalu banyak!)

Please do not tell him the truth!
(Tolong janagn beri tahu dia kebenarannya!)

Do not touch the things, please!
(Jangan menyentuh barang!)

Jawaban:

Instruction...!

1. Please Close the door..!

2. Pass the salt, please..!

Invitation.

1. Are you free this evening? Let's go to the movie.

2. I would like to invite you to come to my party tonight.

1. Don't follow me.

2. Don't disturb me. I'm studying.

3. Don't leave me alone.

Permission.

1. May I borrow your phone, please?

2. Do you mind lending me your money?

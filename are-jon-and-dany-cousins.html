2. Jeane and I are cousins kalimat pertanyaan​


Penjelasan:

ARE YOU COUSINS OF JEANE ?

Jawaban:

(?) are Jeane and I cousins?

Pembahasan

Kalimat soal berstructure:

Subject => Jeane and I Tobe => are Noun => cousins

Berdasarkan hal di atas, kalimat bentuk Present Tense bentuk Nominal Sentence. Karena tidak ada kata kerja.

Rumus yg digunakan

(+) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adj/Adv/Noun

Jeane and I are cousins

(-) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + not + Adj/Adv/Noun

Jeane and I are not cousins

(?) Tobe (is/am/are) + S + Adj/Adv/Noun?

are Jeane and I cousins ?

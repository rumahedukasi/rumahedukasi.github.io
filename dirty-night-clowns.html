31. The bagi is dirty,.....?


Jika ini pembahasan tentang question tag, maka jawabannya adalah..

=> The bag is dirty, isn't it?

Kata it berfungsi sebgai pronoun subject dari the bag.

PEMBAHASAN

Dalam question tag, perhatikan berikut ini :

Apabila kalimat nya positive, maka question tag nya negative.Apabila kalimat nya negative, maka question tag nya positive.

Contoh :

They are students, aren't they?They aren't students, are they?

=================================

Mapel : Bahasa Inggris

Materi : Question Tag

Kelas : 9

Kode Soal : 9.5

Kode Kategorisasi : 9.5.1


32. Jason : "What did you do last weekend?" Carla : "I went to the Circus." Jason : "What was there? BE Carla : ... Based on the picture The right sentence to complete Carla's dialog is. A. There are two clowns in that circus B. There were two clowns in that circus C. There was two clowns in that circus D. There is two clowns in that circus​


Jawaban:

B. There were two clowns in that circus

Penjelasan:

Was merupakan bentuk lampau dari is, sedangkan were merupakan bentuk lampau dari are. Pada kasus ini, di karenakan terdapat dua orang badut di sirkus tersebut, maka kita menggunakan subjek they yang tobe-nya menggunakan are pada present tense & were pada past tense.

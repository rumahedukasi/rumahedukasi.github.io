bahasa Indonesia

1. Pelapukan Kimia

Pelapukan kimia atau khemis adalah terjadi karena adanya suatu reaksi kimia yang menyebabkan hancurnya batuan. Pelapukan jenis ini bisa terjadi dengan cepat di daerah yang sangat panas atau sangat dingin.

Contoh pelapukan kimia

Peristiwa pelapukan kimia bisa terjadi karena batuan bereaksi dengan bahan kimia tertentu, misalnya pada batuan gamping yang melapuk karena terkena air.

2. Pelapukan Fisika

Pelapukan fisika atau mekanik merupakan suatu proses hancurnya batuan karena proses fisika pada batuan tersebut. Pelapukan jenis ini biasanya tidak akan mengubah sebuah sifat dasar dan komposisi batuan yang mengalaminya.

Pelapukan fisika biasanya terjadi karena pada temperatur di sekitar batuan selalu berubah-ubah secara cepat. Peristiwa pelapukan fisika bisa terjadi karena batuan mengalami perubahan mekanik.

Contoh pelapukan fisika

Misalnya pada sebuah batu pada siang hari memuai karena panas matahari dan pada malam hari akan mengerut karena udara dingin.

3. Pelapukan Biologi

Pelapukan biologi atau organik merupakan suatu proses hancurnya batuan karena kegiatan makhluk hidup. Pelapukan biologi biasanya disertai oleh pelapukan kimia.

Contoh pelapukan biologi

Misalnya pada batu yang hancur karena ditumbuhi oleh lumut, dan tanaman lain, atau batu yang berlubang karena dilubangi oleh semut.

bahasa Inggris

1. Chemical weathering

Chemical or chemical decay is caused by a chemical reaction that causes the destruction of rocks. This type of weathering can happen quickly in very hot or very cold regions.

Examples of chemical weathering

Chemical weathering events can occur because rocks react with certain chemicals, for example in weathered limestone due to exposure to water.

2. Weathering Physics

Physical or mechanical weathering is a process of rock destruction because of the physical processes in these rocks. Weathering of this type usually will not change the nature and composition of the rocks that experience it.

Weathering usually happens because the temperature around the rock always changes rapidly. Weathering weathering events can occur because rocks undergo mechanical changes.

Examples of weathering physics

For example, on a rock during the day it expands because of the sun's heat and at night it will shrink because of the cold air.

3. Biological weathering

Biological or organic weathering is a process of rock destruction due to the activity of living things. Biological weathering is usually accompanied by chemical weathering.

Examples of weathering biology

For example in rocks that have been crushed due to overgrown by moss, and other plants, or stones that have holes with holes in them by ants.

21. What is leadership and types


Explanation:

What is leadership? Leadership is the action or ability to lead a group of people to achieve common goals.

There are five types of leadership style, they are:
- The authoritarian leader
this type of leadership style has a full authority to make a decision alone.
- The participative leader
this type of leadership style involves one or more people in decision making process but maintains the final decision making authority.
- The delegative leader
this type of leadership style allows people to make their own decisions but is still responsible for the decisions that have been made.
- The transactional leader
this type of leadership style gives rewards or punishments to team members based on their performance results.
- The transformational leader
this type of leadership style involves high communication skill from management to meet goals.

26. Different types of polynomials


Jawab:

Jenis-jenis Polinomial secara umum, yaitu:

1.) Monomial

⇒ Pernyataan matematika yang terbentuk dalam satu suku saja, seperti 7x², 12, 6a²b³, dan sebagainya.

2.) Binomial

⇒ Polinomial yang bentuknya dinyatakan dalam dua suku, seperti 2x + 4, 3a⁴ + 9b²c, − 10u⁵ − 5v³, dan sebagainya.

3.) Trinomial

⇒ Polinomial yang bentuknya dinyatakan dalam tiga suku, seperti 5x² − x + 6, 8y⁵ − 4y³ + 2, 36x⁷ + x⁴ − 12, dan sebagainya.

∴ Polinomial yang terdiri lebih dari tiga suku tidak memiliki nama khusus.

40. mentions of types of leters


There are several types of letter. Here are some that I can mention:

Thank You letterReference letterApplication letterIntroduction letterSales LetterFormal LetterBirthday LetterEtc.
Pembahasan

1. Thank you letter: often called as a letter of thanks is used to express appreciation of someone’s kindness or simply just a party wishes.


2. Reference letter: also known as letter of recommendation, is a letter that explain and speaks about a person’s working experience, achievements, skills, academic performance, etc. Usually this letter is written by colleague, teacher/lecturer or employer.


3. Application letter: it is a document that usually sent along with curriculum vitae when you’re about to apply for a job. This letter intended to provide further information about how your qualification fits.


4. Introduction letter: this letter has a purpose to introduce an individual or your/someone else’s business to another party.


5. Sales letter: it is a letter that’s written to encourage reader on buying a certain service or product as the replacement of salesman. We can also conclude that this letter is a mail which is sent to potential customer by advertiser.


6. Formal letter: this is a letter written with a formal writing style. It is usually a business letter from one company or organization to another as the part of their correspondence.


7. Birthday letter: nowadays it is more common known as a birthday card. It is a greeting when someone is celebrating a birthday written in a piece of greeting card (depending on the celebration).

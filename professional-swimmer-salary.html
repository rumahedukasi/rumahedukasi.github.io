Salary Crediting merupakan fasilitas yang disediakan bank bagi perusahaan guna pemberian gaji karyawan. 13. Kenny's salary was 30% more than Walter's. Both of their salary increased 50% and the total amount of salary was $6555. Find out Kenny's salary before the increase. Tolong diselesaikan soalnya, trims :)


Jawaban:

Let's solve the problem step by step.

Let K be Kenny's salary before the increase, and W be Walter's salary before the increase.

We are given:

1. Kenny's salary was 30% more than Walter's, which can be written as:

K = W + 0.30W

K = 1.30W

2. Both of their salaries increased by 50%, so after the increase, their salaries can be written as:

Kenny's new salary: 1.50K

Walter's new salary: 1.50W

3. The total amount of salary after the increase was $6555, which can be expressed as:

1.50K + 1.50W = $6555

Now, we can use these equations to solve for Kenny's salary before the increase (K):

Substitute the value of K from the first equation into the second equation:

1.50(1.30W) + 1.50W = $6555

Now, simplify and solve for W:

1.95W + 1.50W = $6555

Combine like terms:

3.45W = $6555

Now, divide both sides by 3.45 to find the value of W (Walter's salary before the increase):

W ≈ $1,900

Now that we have Walter's salary before the increase, we can find Kenny's salary before the increase using the first equation:

K = 1.30W

K = 1.30 * $1,900

K ≈ $2,470

So, Kenny's salary before the increase was approximately $2,470.

21. Noah always saves 50% of his salary. If his salary decreases by 35%, his savings will decrease by $280. How much is Noah's original salary?​


Let's assume Noah's original salary is represented by "x" dollars.

Since Noah always saves 50% of his salary, his savings would be 0.5x dollars.

If his salary decreases by 35%, his new salary would be (1 - 0.35) * x = 0.65x dollars.

Since his savings decreased by $280, we can set up the following equation: 0.5x - 0.5 * 0.65x = $280.

Simplifying the equation, we have: 0.5x - 0.325x = $280.

Combining like terms, we get: 0.175x = $280.

To solve for x, we divide both sides of the equation by 0.175: x = $280 / 0.175.

Performing the calculation, we find that x = $1600.

Therefore, Noah's original salary is $1600.

33. apa itu salary crediting


Salary crediting merupakan salah satu jenis layanan perbankan yang memberikan kemudahan bagi perusahaan untuk membayar gaji karyawan, Perusahaan yang menggunakan salary crediting biasanya merupakan sebuah perusahaan besar, dengan jumlah karyawan ribuan, sehingga perusahaan hanya perlu membuka akun Giro disebuah Bank umum, kemudian semua karyawan juga membuka rekening di bank yang sama, dan perusahaan hanya perlu mengirimkan sofcopy dari jumlah gaji yang harus ditransfer ke masing-masing karyawan, Transaksi ini bisa dilakukan secara realtime dan karyawan bisa mengambil gajinya melalui ATM.

Pembahasan

Kemajuan ilmu pengetahuan dan teknologi akan sangat berpengaruh disegala bidang, begitu juga dengan perbankan, semula kegiatan penggajian dilakukan secara tradisional yaitu dengan cara memberikan gaji tersebut secara langsung, berubah menjadi menggulnakan layanan perbankan.

Mungkin bukan suatu masalah jika perusahaan tersebut merupakan perusahaan kecil yang hanya memiliki sedikit karyawan, namun akan menjadi masalah jika perusahaan tersebut merupakan perusahaan yang besar dengan jumlah karyawan yang ribuan.

Meminimalisir waktu dan tenaga, maka banyak pihak manajemen perusahaan memanfaatkan layanan perbankan, yaitu menggunakan  salary crediting, dengan menggunakan layanan salary crediting maka pekerjaan manajemen keuangan akan lebih mudah.

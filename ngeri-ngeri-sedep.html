Permutasi kata "HASIL NYA" 20,160 susunan kata

===================================

• NGERI

G=1

E=1

Unsur ganda = -

= 5.4.3.2.1!

= 5×4×3×2×1

= 120 susunan kata

• HASIL NYA

H=1

A=2

S=1

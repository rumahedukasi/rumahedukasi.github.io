16. 6 Rewrite the sentences using the verbsin brackets.1 'Congratulations on winning the scholarship,Amandal' said her mum.Amanda's mum___(congratulate)2 I'll help you prepare for the job interview,Karen, said Dave.Dave___(offer)3 Do you want to become a fitness instructor,Dan?' asked Ronny.Ronny___(ask)4 I'm sorry I made a mistake with your flightreservation, the travel agent told me.The travel agent___(apologise)5 Don't forget to fill in the questionnaire, theteacher said to the class.The teacher___(remind)​


REPORTED SPEECH

1. Amanda's mum congratulated Amanda on winning the scholarship

2. Dave offered Karen that he would help her prepare for the job interview

3. Ronny asked Dan if he wanted to become a fitness instructor.

4. The travel agent apologised that he/she had made a mistake with my flight reservation.

5 . The teacher reminded the class not to forget to fill in the questionnaire.

EXPLANATION

Indirect speech (reported speech) merupakan pelaporan ulang ulasan yang disampaikan oleh narasumber, tanpa mengubah makna tersirat dalam pemberitaan di waktu yang berbeda.

Perubahan tenses pada noun clause nya bergantung pada bentuk kata kerja pelaporannya { reporting verb }.

Jika reporting verb-nya berbentuk lampau { said, told, asked }, mengalami backshift :

Simple present=>Simple pastSimple past=>Past perfectPresent perfect=>Past perfectSimple future =>Present conditionalPresent continuous=>Past continuousPast continuous=>Past perfect continuous

Ini dikecualikan jika pernyataan yang disampaikan merupakan jenis informasi faktual, tidak mengalami backshift meskipun reporting verb-nya bentuk lampau.

ELABORATION

1. "Congratulations on winning the scholarship, Amanda !" said her mum.

Reporting verb => congratulated

Maka, bentuk reported speech-nya menjadi :

Amanda's mum congratulated Amanda on winning the scholarship

2. "I'll help you prepare for the job interview,

Karen, said Dave.

Pronoun "I" => "he" Finite verb "will help"=> "would help"Simple Future => Present conditional

Maka, bentuk reported speech-nya :

Dave offered Karen that he would help her prepare for the job interview.

3. "Do you want to become a fitness instructor, Dan?'' asked Ronny."

Y/N question=> whether/if Pronoun "you" => "he" Finite verb "want"=> "wanted"Simple present=> Simple past

Maka, bentuk reported speech-nya :

Ronny asked Dan if he wanted to become a fitness instructor.

4. "I'm sorry I made a mistake with your flight reservation, the travel agent told me.

Pronoun "I => "he/she" Finite verb "made" => "had made"Simple past => Past perfect

Maka, reported speech-nya :

The travel agent apologised that he/she had made a mistake with my flight reservation.

5. "Don't forget to fill in the questionnaire, the

teacher said to the class."

Negative command=>negative infinitive

Maka, reported speech-nya mjd :

The teacher reminded the class not to forget to fill in the questionnaire.

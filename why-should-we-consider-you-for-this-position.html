Hereby I attached my  complete CV and letters of recommendation.

Thank you for considering my applications and I always look for your interview.

Sincerely, Scott Lang.

28. 3. You are a fresh graduate and saw a job vacancy/opening on a website for a librarian at an international school. Write a cover letter in English stating: - who you are, - the position you are applying to, - where you found the opening/vacancy, - your interest in the position, - reasons why you are a good fit to the position, and - thank you for the time to consider you.


Example for cover letter in English:

Dear Principal school,

I am writing to express my strong interest in the librarian position at SDN Kampung Baru 4 as advertised on your website. As a recent graduate with a passion for literature and a keen interest in working in an educational environment, I am excited about the opportunity to contribute to the learning experience at your esteemed international school.

Allow me to introduce myself. My name is Rina Maulida, and I have recently completed my Bachelor's degree in Library Science from Airlangga University. Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a deep understanding of library management, information organization, and the promotion of literacy. I am equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in a librarian role.

I discovered the opening for the librarian position through your school's website. I was immediately drawn to the opportunity due to my personal values aligning with those, and the emphasis your institution places on fostering a love for reading and knowledge. I firmly believe that access to a well-curated library, along with a dedicated librarian, can inspire students to become lifelong learners and critical thinkers.

What makes me a strong fit for this position is my combination of theoretical knowledge and practical experience gained through internships and academic projects. During my time at Airlangga University. I actively participated in library outreach programs, assisted students in conducting research, and organized literary events. These experiences have not only honed my organizational and communication skills but have also reinforced my commitment to creating an inclusive and engaging library environment.

Moreover, I possess excellent research skills, a deep understanding of digital resources, and a strong ability to adapt to new technologies and library management systems. I believe that my technological proficiency, coupled with my enthusiasm for embracing innovation, will enable me to enhance the library services at SDN Kampung Baru 4 and provide students with a cutting-edge learning experience.

Thank you for considering my application. I am eager to contribute my knowledge, skills, and passion for promoting literacy within the international school community. I would appreciate the opportunity to discuss in more detail how my qualifications align with your school's objectives during an interview.

Please find enclosed my resume, which provides further information on my educational background and relevant experiences. I look forward to the possibility of joining the dedicated team at SDN Kampung Baru 4 and making a positive impact on the intellectual development of your students. Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,

Rina Maulida

Pembahasan

Surat lamaran kerja adalah sebuah dokumen tertulis yang digunakan untuk mengajukan permohonan atau melamar pekerjaan di sebuah perusahaan atau organisasi. Surat ini berfungsi sebagai pengantar dan memberikan informasi tentang diri kita, kualifikasi, dan minat kita terhadap posisi yang dilamar.

Pastikan untuk menyusun surat lamaran dengan rapi, menjaga bahasa yang sopan dan profesional, serta menjelaskan dengan jelas mengapa kita cocok untuk posisi yang dilamar. Selain itu, sertakan juga resume atau CV yang mendukung lamaran kita.

Example of a cover letter in English

Dear Hiring Manager,

My name is Jennie Juby Jane. I am 26 years old and from Seoul, South Korea.

I am writing to express my interest in the Content Writer position that you have available. Your job posting on LinkedIn for a Content Writer Intern piqued my interest. Your description of the work responsibilities for the Content Writer Intern role closely matches my experience, and so I am excited to submit my resume for your consideration.

I have been working as a content writer for a year. I have worked with clients to develop their online presence and create strategies to increase their visibility. I can write in different styles depending on the needs of the client. I can write in a formal style or a casual style, depending on what is needed.

I am confident that my experience as a content writer makes me an ideal candidate for this position. I am also confident that my skills will allow me to be successful in this position. I would appreciate the opportunity to discuss my qualifications and how they can benefit your company. Thank you for your time.

Sincerely,

Jennie Juby Jane

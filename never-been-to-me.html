Berto and Adam asked me if I had ever been to England before.


Jawaban:

A. Berto and Adam asked me whether I have ever been to England before

B. Berto and Adam asked me if they had ever been to England before.

C. Berto and Adam asked me whether he has ever been to England before.

D. Berto and Adam asked me if I had ever been to England before.

Reported Speech, dalam bahasa Indonesia artinya adalah kalimat tidak langsung. Perubahan kalimat dari Direct Speech menjadi Reported Speech memiliki aturan:

Present Perfect Tense

He asked me, "have you been to Spain?"

He asked me if I had been to Spain.

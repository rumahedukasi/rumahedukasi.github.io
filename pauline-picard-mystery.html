21. Keddy likes playing math games with his father. His father wrote a mystery number and said, I will get 1.3 when I divide the mystery number by 5.How many tenths are there in this mystery number?


[tex] \frac{x}{5} = 1.3 \\ \\ x = 1.3 \times 5 \\ x = 6.5 = \frac{65}{10} = 65 \: tenths[/tex]

There are 65 tenths in the mistery number.

 

Explanation

Let [tex]x[/tex] be the mistery number, which satisfies:

[tex]\begin{aligned}x\div 5&=1.3\\\Rightarrow x&=1.3\times5\\\therefore\ x&=6.5\end{aligned}[/tex]

The question is “how many tenths are there in [tex]x[/tex]?”. Thus, [tex]x[/tex] also satisfies:

[tex]\begin{aligned}x&=k\times\frac{1}{10}\\\end{aligned}[/tex]

Solving it:

[tex]\begin{aligned}\Rightarrow6.5&=k\times\frac{1}{10}\\\Rightarrow\ \ k&=10\times6.5\\\therefore\ k&=\boxed{\ \bf65\ }\end{aligned}[/tex]

 

CONCLUSION

∴  There are 65 tenths in the mistery number.

33. raj design a game "the mystery number" to help his friends in his study group learn maths. in this game 19 is added to the mystery number.the result is multiplied by 2. after that 357 is then subtracted. the final value is 3451. what was the mystery number at thr begining??​


Jawab: 1.885

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

let's call the mystery number as "a"

(a + 19) 2 - 357 = 3.451

2a + 38 - 357 = 3.451

2a - 319 = 3.451

2a = 3.451 + 319

2a = 3.770

a = 3.770/2

a = 1.885

So, the mystery number at the beginning of the game is 1.885.

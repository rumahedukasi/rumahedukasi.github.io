12. change the sentences into negative and introgrrative form!example:Jack is boxing on tv now(-).Jack is not boxing on tv now(?). is Jack boxing on tv now1.I'am coming home.(-)(?)2.chris is wariting a letter.(-)(?)3.the children are playing volleyball.(-)(?)4.she is wearing a new hat.(-)(?)5.i'am eating a cake.i swimming pool.(-)(?)​


Jawaban:

Present Continuous Tense:

1. (+) I am coming home.

(-) I am not coming home.

(?) Am I coming home? / Are you coming home?

2. (+) Chris is writing a letter.

(-) Chris is not waiting a letter.

(?) Is Chris writing a letter?

3. (+) The children are playing volleyball.

(-) The children are not playing volley ball.

(?) Are the children playing volley ball?

4. (+) She is wearing a new hat.

(-) She is not wearing a new hat.

(?) Is she wearing a new hat?

5. (+) I am eating a cake.

(-) I am not eating a cake.

(?) Am I eating a cake?

6. (+) I am swimming in a swimming pool.

(-) I am not swimming in a swimming pool.

(?)​ Am I swimming in a swimming pool?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Present Continuous Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan yang sedang dilakukan dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Present Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Verb-ing + Adverb of time (Now/today)

(-) Subejct + Tobe (is/am/are) + not + Verb-ing + Adverb of time (Now/today)

(?) Tobe (is/am/are) + Subject + Verb-ing + Adverb of time (Now/today)?

Terkadang Adverb of Time juga disebut sebagai Time Signal.

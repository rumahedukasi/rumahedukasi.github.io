5. Tolong buat kan dulu percakapan antara penjual dan pembeli tentang membeli laptop beserta kualitas laptop nya


Dialog Between Seller and Customer on Buying New LaptopJawaban
Pendahuluan

Pada soal diminta untuk menuliskan dialog yang berhubungan tentang membeli sebuah barang. Di dalam percakapan harus tercantum kalimat yang membahas tentang kualitasnya. Sehingga ada beberapa pertanyaan umum yang dapat digunakan dalam dialog ini:

How much it cost? (Berapa harganya?)What about the quality? (Bagaimana kualitasnya?)Please explain me about the specification (Tolong jelaskan tentang spesifikasi komputernya)Is there any guarantee? (Apakah ada garansi?)Where is the service center available? (Dimana tersedia tempat servis?
Pembahasan

Customer: Hello, I am looking for a Macbook Air in the year of 2016. Is it still available?

Seller: I'm sorry ma'am, we are currently out of stock. But I can offer you the other version of Macbook Air which is the newest. Produced in 2017.

Customer: Hmmm. How much does it cost?

Seller: This one costs USD1000.

Customer: Wow that is a lot of money! What about the quality? Because I'm not paying such amount for a low specification.

Seller: Okay ma'am I will explain to you. This Macbook Air has 1.8GHz dual-core Intel Core i5 processor which can be boost up to 2.9GHz. The RAM itself is 8GB and it has Intel HD Graphics 600. The storage you can choose between 128 and 256GB. The size is very thin and slim, the battery is up to 12 hours without charging. Overall this is the cheapest and the best computer from Apple for now.

Customer: Would you recommend this product?

Seller: Absolutely, ma'am. Apple always make a great quality product that lasts for years. My Macbook has been there for 5 years and still working great.

Customer: Hmm, okay. So I am taking this one.

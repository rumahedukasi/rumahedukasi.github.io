24. contoh kalimat gerund sebagai subject,object dan pelengkap.minimal 3


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: gerund vs infinitives
Pembahasan:

Gerund is a word in the form of noun, which is transformed from a verb, by adding -ing at the back of it, for instance: playing, dancing, talking etc.
Gerund can be used as a subject, object or as a complement.

a. as a subject of the sentence
    - Playing football has been their routine every saturday afternoon.
    - Reading has much more advantage than you know it.
    - Fishing in the river isn't as easy as you imagine.

b. as an object ( can be after a preposition, which is preceded by an adjective or after a verb)
- She's keen on learning languages (after a preposition 'on')
- They always fancy going camping. (after a verb 'fancy')
- I detest diving. (after a verb 'detest')

c. as a complement
    The sport I'm not really into is swimming actually.
    The stuff they did all day was just chatting.
    The thing she has done to me so far is just whining.

Gerund adalah kata kerja yang ditambahi akhir -ing sehingga fungsinya menjadi kata benda. Beberapa fungsi gerund antara lain adalah sebagai subjek, objek, noun modifier, complement, preposition, possessive, dll.

Contoh penggunaan gerund sebagai subjek:

Watching movie is my hobbie. (Watching sebagai subjek kalimat)

Gerund sebagai objek:

My hobbie is watching. (Watching sebagai objek kalimat)

Gerund sebagai noun modifier:

The waiting room is full of men. (Waiting room sebagai noun modifier)

Gerund sebagai complement:

One of her duties is cleaning the floor. (Cleaning sebagai complement)

37. What is the function of gerund as subject and as object? Explain it with sentences examples! pake inggris+translete yaa kak​


Jawaban: What Is a Gerund?

Gerunds (pronounced jer-unds) are verbs that end in -ing but function as nouns. They keep verb-like qualities, such as using adverbs or taking objects.

Like nouns, gerunds can function in various ways in a sentence.

Running is Olivia’s favorite activity. (Subject)

Olivia loves running around her block. (Direct object)

Olivia spends her free time running. (Indirect object)

Olivia even dreams about running. (Object of the preposition)

Olivia’s passion is running. (Predicate noun)

Translate : Apa Itu Gerund?

Gerund (diucapkan jer-unds) adalah kata kerja yang diakhiri dengan -ing tetapi berfungsi sebagai kata benda. Mereka menjaga kualitas seperti kata kerja, seperti menggunakan kata keterangan atau mengambil objek.

Seperti kata benda, gerund dapat berfungsi dengan berbagai cara dalam sebuah kalimat.

Berlari adalah aktivitas favorit Olivia. (Subjek)

Olivia suka berlarian di bloknya. (Objek langsung)

Olivia menghabiskan waktu luangnya dengan berlari. (Objek tidak langsung)

Olivia bahkan bermimpi untuk berlari. (Objek preposisi)

Gairah Olivia sedang berjalan. (Kata benda predikat)

= √3/3 x 2 x √3/2 + 8 x 1/2
= 1 + 4

1. c.
(6 cos 45°)(sin 60°) + (6 cos 45°)(sin 30°)
= 6 x √2/2 x √3/2 + 6 x √2/2 x 1/2
= 3 √2 x √3/2 + 3 √2 x 1/2
= 3/2 √6 + 3/2 √2
= 3/2 (√6 + √2)

28. Change to be passive voice!We’ll send the parcel as soon as we get it.​


1. The parcel will be sent to you by us as soon as we get it

Discussion

Passive Voice is a sentence whose subject comes before the predicate. Passive voice is a sentence whose suject is subject to an action.

The characteristics of the Passive sentence, that is:

The subject is a sufferer.The predicate is affixed with di- and ter-.The predicate is in the form of a personal predicate.

Completion question

1. We'll send the parcel as soon as we get it

==> The parcel will be sent to you by us as soon as we get it

In the question is a Passive Simple Future sentence.

Conclusion

From the question above, we know that:

This question used the Passive Simple Future sentence.Used the formula: S + Will + be + V3

29. jelaskan fugsi dari • Bring To Front • Bring Forward • Send Backward • Send To Back


Hai.

Apa ini? Terserah.

Bring to front berfungsi membawa objek melangkahi satu lapisan ke depan, sedangkan send to back sebaliknya yaitu membawa objek melangkahi satu lapisan ke belakang. Bring forward berfungsi untuk membawa objek melangkahi lapisan terdepan, sedangkan send backward sebaliknya.

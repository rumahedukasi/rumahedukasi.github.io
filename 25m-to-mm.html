1) 2.500 km - 2.500 dam = ... km
» 2.500 km = 2.500 km
» 2.500 dam = 2.500 : 100 = 25 km
» 2.500 km - 25 km = 2.475 km

2) 20 m + 120 dm - 6 dam = ... dam
» 20 m = 20 : 10 = 2 dam
» 120 dm = 120 : 100 = 1,2 dam
» 6 dam = 6 dam
» 2 dam + 1,2 dam - 6 dam = -2,8 dam

3) 1.500 mm + 25 m = ... mm
» 1.500 mm = 1.500 mm
» 25 m = 25 × 1000 = 25.000 mm
» 1.500 mm + 25.000 mm = 26.500 mm

4) 6,2 km - 22 hm - 3.570 m = ... m
» 6,2 km = 6,2 × 1000 = 6.200 m
» 22 hm = 22 × 100 = 2.200 m
» 3.570 m = 3.570 m
» 6.200 m - 2.200 m - 3.570 m = 430 m

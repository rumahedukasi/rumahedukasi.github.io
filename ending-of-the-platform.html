9. 1. Find the story in sad ending! a. How are the characters? b.What is the moral lesson of the story?​


Nih cerita ku buat sendiri

Once upon the time, there's live a girl named Siti in a noble family. Her mother married to a new husband which is richer and make her title better. She live with her mother, step-father and step-sister. Unfortunately, one day her step-father passed away, his title and glory passed to her mother.

Her step-sister felt really depressed because she dont have her biological father and mother anymore, she once sit near the window to kill herself. Siti who was passing at that time shocked and told her not to do it but then her step-sister said "Why do you care? You and your mother always being mean to me anyway." Siti startled and dissapointed with her response so she said "Yeah, you deserve to die, i hate you." Hearing that sentence her step-sister reply "After hearing those words, now im not in mood to die."

12 years later and they are already grown up into an adult in marriageable age. Siti fall in love with one of the duke in the kingdom but she is rejected by the duke, she feels heartbroken but obsessed with the duke. One day, her step-sister accidentally meet the duke and having a good love story with him, they both fall in love and when Siti know it she get jealous and tried to scare her sister by throwing glass near her. But, when she want to do it, suddenly the duke came in the house with some guards and arrest Siti, her mother was summoned infront of the judge to watch. The duke use his power to make Siti to have a great punishment because he is really mad with what she did. The judge finally tell what punishment she deserve, she is sentenced to death. Siti shocked and scream to her mother who watch "Mother! I didnt do anything wrong! Mother u supported me when i was falling in love with the duke and even suggested me a lot of things to get rid of my step-sister why dont u say anything?" Her mother scared but start to make an act to protect herself "Silence! You are no longer my daugther! Dont drag me into this problem!" After hearing that she start crying and glare to her step-sister, "Sister! All i want is happiness and love in my life! I only want to scare you and i dont have intention to harm you! Why dont you atleast have a pity of me who lived years with you? None believe me since the beginning! If you are hurt the one who take a blame is me since i look like a villainess in your life!" Her step-sister kept silence and stare him with a cold stare. Siti dragged by the guard and sentenced to death at that time.

Jawaban pertanyaan

1. The main character is Siti, she is a person who look like a vilainess in a story because her status as the daugther of someone who married to a man with higher title. Meanwhile her mother and step-sister both are the antagonist characters of the story both of their role is to give the bad ending to Siti.

2. The moral of the story is, not everyone that look like a bad person is actually a bad person. We shouldn't judge anyone from their status.

Fairy Tale atau Dongeng atau Cerita Rakyat (Folk Tale) merupakan cerita yang berasal dari suatu daerah yang biasanya berdasarkan dari hal-hal yang bersifat supranatural, dan mistik.

Dongeng biasanya difahami sebagai cerita yang tidak benar-benar akan terjadi di dunia nyata.

Unsur- unsur dalam dongeng, terdapat 2 unsur, yakni Unsur intrinsik yang membentuk cerita dongeng berasal dari dalam, unsur intrinsik dongeng antara lain tema, amanat, latar, sudut pandang, tokoh, penokohan, alur.

Unsur ekstrinsik merupakan unsur yang membentuk cerita dongeng dari luar dan unsur ekstrinsik meliputi latar belakang masyarakat, latar belakang pengarang.

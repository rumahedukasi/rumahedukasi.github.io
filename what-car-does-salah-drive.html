Rumus Simple Present Tense;
(+) Subject(He/She/It/Kata benda tunggal) + V1-es/-s + O
(-) Subject(He/She/It/Kata benda tunggal) + does not + V1 + O
(?) Does + Subject(He/She/It/Kata benda tunggal) + V1 + O

(+) Father drives his car to his office every wednesday.
(-) Father does not drive his car to his office every wednesday.
(?) Does father drive his car to his office every wednesday?

27. A: What cant Anton do? B: Oh, he .... to drive a car​


Jawaban:

A: "What can't Anton do?"

B: "Oh, he can't drive a car!"

Penjelasan:

Ability = kebisaan seseorang melakukan sesuatu

Rule grammarnya sebagai berikut:

[+] Subject + can + verb + object

[-] Subject + can't + verb + object.

[?+] Can + subject + verb + object?

[?-] Can't + subject + verb + object?

Contohnya Subject = he, verb = drive (regular verb), object = car.

[+] He can drive a car = dia bisa nyetir mobil

[-] He can't drive a car = dia tidak bisa nyetir mobil

[?+] Can he drive a car? = Bisakah dia menyetir mobil?

[?-] Can't he drive a car? = Apa dia tidak bisa nyetir mobil?

39. What is the modal's function of ... In this sentence i can drive a car, but i can't drive a motorcycle


Jawaban:

The function of the modal verb "can/can't" dalam kalimat "I can drive a car, but I can't drive a motorcycle" is to identify ability/inability. Dalam Bahasa Indonesia, itu artinya untuk menyatakan kebisaan atau ketidakbisaan. Modal verb "can" artinya adalah "bisa/dapat" sedangkan "can't/cannot" artinya adalah "tidak bisa/tidak dapat"

Penjelasan:

Penggunaan modal verb "can"

Dalam kalimat "I can drive a car", dapat diartikan bahwa "Saya dapat mengemudikan mobil. Sehingga, modal verb "can" dapat diartikan sebagai cara untuk mengekspresikan "ability" "kebisaan".

Contoh lain:

She can speak in 3 languages/ Dia dapat berbicara dalam 3 bahasa.

We can play video games after finishing our homework/Kita dapat main video games setelah selesai mengerjakan PR.

Penggunaan modal verb "can't/cannot"

Sebaliknya, penambahan kata "not" kepada modal verb "can" menyatakan ketidakbisaan/inability. Sehingga kalimat "but I can't drive a motorcycle" berarti "tapi saya tidak bisa mengemudikan motor"

Contoh lain:

We cannot go to school because of the pandemic/Kita tidak bisa pergi sekolah karena pandemi.

She can't play the piano/ Dia tidak bisa main piano.

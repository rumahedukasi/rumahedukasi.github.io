27. K u i s .V o l u m e s e b u a h T a b u n g t a n p a t u t u p a d a l a h 5250 liter. d e n g a n t i n g g i t a b u n g t e r s e b u t 20,5 dm. m a k a h i t u n g l a h l u a s p e r m u k a a n t a b u n g t e r s e b u t!​


Jawaban*

Lp = 1.416,70 dm²

Penjelasan Rinci*

Diketahui

V = 5250 liter (dm³)

t = 20,5 dm

Ditanya

Lp tanpa tutup?

Jawab

V = π × r² × t

[tex]r = \sqrt{ \frac{V}{\pi \times t} } [/tex]

[tex]r \: = \: \sqrt{ \frac{5250}{ \frac{22}{7} \times 20.5} } [/tex]

[tex]r \: = \: \sqrt{81.48} [/tex]

[tex]r \: = \: 9.02 \: dm[/tex]

Maka Luas permukaannya adalah

[tex]Lp \: = \: \pi \times r(r + 2 \times t)[/tex]

[tex]Lp = 3.14 \times 9.02 \: (9.02 + 2 \times 20.5)[/tex]

[tex]Lp = 1416.70 \: \: \: {dm}^{2} [/tex]

Terimakasih

Geometri Bangun Ruang

Diketahui bahwa :

Volume tabung tanpa tutup = 5.250 liter = 5.250 dm³Tinggi tabung = 20,5 dm

Jari-Jari Alas Tabung :

▪ [tex]\large V = \pi r^2 t[/tex]

▪ [tex]\large 5.250 = 3,14 × 20,5 × r^2[/tex]

▪ [tex]\large 5.250 = 64,37~r^2[/tex]

▪ [tex]\large r = \sqrt{\displaystyle \frac{5.250}{64,37}} [/tex]

▪ [tex]\large r = \sqrt{81,55} [/tex]

▪ [tex]\large r = 9,03~\sf{dm}[/tex]

Luas permukaan tabung tanpa tutup

▪ [tex]\large Lp = \pi r × (r + 2t)[/tex]

▪ [tex]\large Lp = 3,14 × 9,03 × [9,03 + (2 × 20,5)] [/tex]

▪ [tex]\large Lp = 3,14 × 9,03 × (9,03 + 41) [/tex]

▪ [tex]\large Lp = 3,14 × 9,03 × 50,03 [/tex]

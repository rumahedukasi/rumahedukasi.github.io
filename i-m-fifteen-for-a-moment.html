36. susy ( play ) badminton for fifteen minutes


Soal:

Susy (play) badminton for fifteen minutes

Jawab:

(+) S + has/have + V3 + O/C

= Susy has played badminton for fifteen minutes

Pembahasan:

* Karena dalam soal terdapat Time Signal FOR yg berarti SELAMA maka gunakan Rumus Present Perfect Tense

(+) S + has/have + V3 + O/C

= Susy has played badminton for fifteen minutes

(Susy telah bermain bulu tangkis selama lima belas menit)

* Alasan menggunakan HAS karena Subjek tunggal SUSY yg memiliki kata ganti SHE (dia perempuan)

* Has = He, She, it

* Have = I, You, we, they

* V1 play = V2 played = V3 played

==== Semoga Membantu ====

Mapel: Bahasa Inggris

Kelas: SMP

Materi: Present Perfect

Kode Mapel: 5

Jawaban: COOKS

Yang ditanyakan Find the mistakes (temukan kesalahan) ?

(+) My mother cooks in the kitchen yesterday. ❌

(-) My mother didn't cook in the kitchen yesterday.✅

(?) Did my mother cooks in the kitchen yesterday?❌

Penjelasan:

Berdasarkan kalimat pada soal terdapat kata YESTERDAY (kemarin) maka kalimat tersebut adalah PAST TENSE yaitu kejadian masa lampau.

Kalimat yg SEHARUSNYA Berdasarkan Rumus Past Tense:

(+) S + VERB 2 + O

= My mother COOKED in the kitchen yesterday. ✅

(-) S + DIDN’t/DID NOT + VERB 1 + O

= My mother didn't COOK + in the kitchen yesterday.✅

(?) DID + S + VERB 1 + O

= Did my mother COOK in the kitchen yesterday?✅

Catatan tentang PAST TENSE:

(+) pada kalimat Positive menggunakan VERB 2 = COOKED

(-) (?) tapi pada kalimat Negative dan Interrogative menggunakan VERB 1 = COOK karena sudah ada DID

JADI:

Yang salah pada SOAL adalah kata kerja/ VERB = COOKS.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat di atas mengikuti aturan kalimat Verbal Simple Past Tense.

Rumus Simple Past Tense:

Kalimat Verbal Positif:

(+)· Subject + verb 2 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(+) We attended the festival last week

Kalimat Nominal Positif:

(+) Subject + to be (was/were) + adjective/adverb (yesterday / last week)

(+) She was absent yesterday

Kalimat Verbal Negatif:

(-) Subject + did not + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(-) We did not attend the festival last week

Kalimat Nominal Negatif:

(-) Subject + was/were + not + adjective/adverb + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(-) She was not absent yesterday

Kalimat Verbal Interogatif:

(?) Did + subject + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)?

(?) Did we attend the festival last week?

Kalimat Nominal Interogatif:

(?) Was/were + subject + adjective/adverb + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)?

(?) Was she absent yesterday?

38. Cahaya:what did you...yesterday,bulan? bulan:oh,i...my grandma in the vilage yesterday.


[Past Simple]

dovisited

Cahaya: "What did you do yesterday, Bulan?"

Bulan: "Oh, I visited my grandma in the vilage yesterday."

PEMBAHASAN

Past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan atau menyebut suatu peristiwa yang telah terjadi pada masa lalu.

formula⬎verbal sentence:

(+) subject + verb 2 + O / C

(-) subject + did + not + verb 1 + O / C

(?) did + subject + verb 1 + O / C + ?

nominal sentence:

(+) subject + was/were + O / C

(-) subject + was/were + not + O / C

(?) was/were + subject + verb 1 + O / C + ?

↬based on question

Fill the blanks!

[dialogue]

Cahaya: "What did you __ yesterday, Bulan?"

Bulan: "Oh, I __ my grandma in the vilage yesterday."

—› arti

Cahaya: "Apa yang kamu __ kemarin, Bulan?"

Bulan: "Oh, aku __ nenekku di desa kemarin."

Untuk mengisi bagian kosong, sesuaikan kata kerja dengan topik yang sedang dibahas. Berdasarkan dialog, kata kerja yang tepat adalah 'lakukan' dan 'mengunjungi'.

Cahaya: "Apa yang kamu lakukan kemarin, Bulan?"

Bulan: "Oh, aku mengunjungi nenekku di desa kemarin."

Karena kalimatnya termasuk past tense (membicarakan kejadian masa lalu) maka verb yang digunakan yaitu dalam bentuk verb 2.

verbs:

(1) do - did - done

melakukan

(2) visit - visited - visited

mengunjungi

"Apa yang kamu lakukan kemarin, Bulan?"

→ "What did you do yesterday, Bulan?"

kata kerja yang digunakan adalah dalam bentuk verb 1 karena kalimatnya introgatif.

"Oh, aku mengunjungi nenekku di desa kemarin."

→ Oh, I visited my grandma in the vilage yesterday.

kata kerja yang digunakan tetap dalam bentuk verb 2 karena kalimatnya positif.

—› completed dialogue

Cahaya: "What did you do yesterday, Bulan?"

Bulan: "Oh, I visited my grandma in the vilage yesterday."

4. After doing yoga, Jessica is accustomed to have a glass of lemon water to detox her body. Choose one word underlined that is not suitable for standard English or Error Recognition


After doing yoga, Jessica is accustomed to have a glass of lemon water to detox her body.

Choose one word underlined that is not suitable for standard English or Error Recognition.

ANSWER: have
------------
EXPLANATION
'be accustomed to' is a phrase that is followed by a noun, a pronoun or gerund.

'have' is neither a noun nor a gerund. it is a bare infinitive.

the correct sentence should be 'after doing yoga, Jessica is accustomed to having a glass of lemon water to detox your body.'
--------------
DETAILS
Subject English
Category: structure and written expressions
Subcategory gerund
Level: SHS
Code: 5

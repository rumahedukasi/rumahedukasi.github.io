32. 9. Nayla I've never been there beforeNazwa Take the bakerloo line from OxfordCircus and change to the northernline at Charing Cross. Get off theKinningtonNayla Just a moment, let me take this down!a. I wonder whether you would care to Kennington by tubeb. Please listen to my trip to Kennington by tubec. Do you know how to get to Kennington by tube?d. Would you mind possibly taking me to kennington by tube?Tolong dijawab, artikan, Jelaskan​


DIALOGUE COMPREHENSION

Nayla: Do you know how to get to Kennington by tube? (c)

ーーーーー

EXPLANATION

Pada percakapan di atas, Nayla bertanya pada Nazwa bagaimana cara pergi ke Kennington dengan kereta bawah tanah karena diketahui dari jawaban Nazwa.

Nazwa memberi tahu Nayla kereta mana yang harus diambil (take the Waterloo Line), di mana harus berganti kereta (at Charing Cross) dan di mana harus turun (get off at Kennington).

Maka jawaban yang tepat adalah opsi c karena

opsi a. I wonder whether you would care to Kennington by tube ➩ Nayla mengajak pergi ke Kenningon dengan kereta bawah tanah.

opsi b. Please listen to my trip to Kennington by tube ➩Nayla meminta Nazwa mendengarkan pengalamannya pergi ke Kennington.

d. Would you mind possibly taking me to kennington by tube? ➩Nayla meminta Nazwa mengantarnya ke Kennington.

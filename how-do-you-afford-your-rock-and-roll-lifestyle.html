22. Ciri ciri musik latin, klasik, jazz, rock, slow rock, hard rock, dan rock and roll


latin = menggunakan gitar2an seperti flamenco
klasik = musiknya digunakan untuk dansa
jazz= kaya akan improvisasi
rock = aliran musik yang keras
slow rock = lebih halus dari rock
hard rock = lebih keras dari rock tapi tidak sekeras metal
rock and roll = musiknya lebih mendayuu2

1. Ciri – ciri Musik Klasik :

Penggunaan dinamika dari Keras menjadi Lembut, Crassendo dan Decrasscendo.Perubahan tempo dengan accelerando (semakin Cepat) dan Ritarteando (semakin lembut).Pemakaian Ornamentik dibatasiPenggunaan Akord 3 nada


 2.Ciri – ciri musik Jazz :

1) Vokal dan lirik cenderung dianggap sebagai bagian dari bunyi instrumen

2) Harmoninya rumit, memiliki tonalitas yang luas dan sering terjadi modulasi

3) Ritme dan melodi memiliki kecenderungan improvisatif.


3.Ciri -ciri musik rock

Aliran musik populer dibedakan menjadi beberapa jenis, salah satunya adalah aluran rock, dengan ciri sebagai berikut:

· Wilayah nada luas dari nada rendah hingga tinggi
· Kekuatan musik pada dinamika aransemen
· Lagu kadang sulit disenandungkan
· Lirik lagu cenderung ekspresi
· Tempo bisa lambat bisa cepat
· Harmoni bisa sangat rumit
· Beat cenderung keras

24. Complete the following transactional conversations. Using the role-play approach, reenact the conversation with your classmates. • Songs A : What kind of music do you like? B : I like rock and roll. A : How can you like rock and roll? It is so loud. A : B : A : B :


For completing the following transactional conversation, we have to understand the definition of transactional conversation. There are two functions of conversation in English that are known as transactional conversation and interactional conversation. Interactional conversation is the function of conversation which focus on making and maintaining social relations.

Transactional conversation is the function of conversation which mainly focus on exchanging information. Conversation in a transactional way is also a way for obtaining information. For example:

E: What kind of food do you like?

C: I really like Korean Food.

E: Isn't it spicy?

C: Yes, I like spicy food.

E: I want to try Korean Food but I don't really like spicy food.

C: You can try Korean food that's not spicy such as Sam Ge Tang which also known ginseng chicken soup. It's not spicy at all.

E: Wow! I want to try it.

C: Let's go to Korean restaurant next Sunday.

In the dialogue, C is giving E information about Korean food that is not spicy because E seems interested to try Korean food but doesn't like it if it's spicy.

Answer

To complete the following transactional conversations, we're going to use rock and roll as the topic. A doesn't understand why B likes rock and roll. We can complete the following transactional conversation to explain that rock and roll is not always loud.

A: What kind of music do you like?

B: I like rock and roll.

A: How can you like rock and roll? It is so loud.

B: I like rock and roll because it's relaxing for me. Rock and roll can be loud but there is also rock and roll music that is not so loud.

A: Oh really? I've always thought that rock and roll is so loud.

B: There are actually some types of rock and roll genre for example classic rock and rock. I think that classic rock is not so loud compared to rock.

A: Wow! I didn't know about that.

B: If you want to try listening to classic rock, you can come to my house. I'll show you my father's collection. He really likes rock and roll so he has a lot of rock and roll music at home.

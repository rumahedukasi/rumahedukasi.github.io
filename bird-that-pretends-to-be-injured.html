17. ini tentang conditional sentence type 3, soalnya if you ... (be) a bird, where ... you... (fly) to?​


CONDITIONAL SENTENCE

Answer :

If you were a bird, where would you fly to?  

=> It should be written in the second conditional because it's impossible to happen and it's just an imagination/illusion.

If you had been a bird, where would you have flown to?

=> If you use the third conditional, it's related to the past  you couldn't reach.

Explanation

Conditional sentence structure consists -If clause and result clause ( main clause ).

Types of Conditional Sentences

ZERO CONDITIONAL

The Zero Conditional is used to describe the Phenomenon, habit, and General truth ( Fact ).

Pattern :

If+S+verb (s/es)+O , S+verb (s/es)+O

Example :

Unless it snows, the pavements are not Slippery.

Discussion :

It's general truth. It is proven that the pavements are slippery when it snows.

FIRST CONDITIONAL

The First Conditional is used to describe the truth in the present/future ( The prediction/planning will be probably done. However, the speaker will actualize his/her dream now, soon or later.

Pattern :

If +S+ verb (s/es) + O, S+will+verb1+O

Example :

She will go abroad if she gets the scholarship.

Discussion :

It uses simple future form in its main clause / result clause to describe the possible condition and state the prediction will be true. It means that she will probably go abroad.

SECOND CONDITIONAL

The second Conditional type is used to describe the untruth in the present / future. It is contrary to the fact in the present or it could be in the future. The speaker has an intention ( dream ) but he/she can't afford his/her dream comes true in the present/future"Keep in mind, if we use the first , second person and third person singular ( I, she, he, it, ) in the nominal sentence , it must be followed by the PAST linking verb "were."" It is the same way to the plural form as the formal writing." If we use the PAST linking verb "was, " it should be used in the colloquial as the informal conversation.

Pattern:

If+S+verb2+O/C , S+would/could+verb1+O/C

Example :

If I were not a teacher, I couldn't teach you.If you didn't go, I would be very angry.If you  were a millionaire, what would you do ?

Discussion :

It uses the simple past form to reflect the untruth condition in the present and the speaker's intention will not come true because it is contrary to the real condition for now or maybe future.

THIRD CONDITIONAL

The Third Conditional is used to describe the untruth in the past. It is contrary to the fact in the past and It reflects a regret for something that the speaker couldn't afford to do in the past.

Pattern :

If+S+Had+Verb 3+O , S+Would/could+have+Verb 3+O

Example :

I wouldn't have missed the bus if we had gone earlier.I'm sure he would have told you if he had broken itIf the accident hadn't happened, what would they have done ?

Discussion :

It reflects the regret for something that couldn't be reached in the past.

28. The opossum is able to confuse its predators and escape from death by to be dead. (A) to pretend (B) it pretends (C) the pretence of (D) pretending


Jawaban yang tepat adalah D. pretending. Jika dimasukkan ke dalam kalimat pertanyannya menjadi "The opossum is able to confuse its predators and escape from death by pretending to be dead. Soal ini ada hubungannya dengan prepostion "by".

PEMBAHASAN:

Adapun beberapa fungsi dari penggunaan kata "by" diantaranya:

Menunjukkan jarak. Penggunaan by yang pertama adalah untuk menunjukkan jarak atau rentang, dan bisa diartikan sebagai kedekatan dari segi dimensi. Misalnya seperti dalam contoh berikut: He was by my side and I could see his face clearly! By dalam kalimat pasif. Kata by sangat erat kaitannya dalam konteks kalimat pasif, yang digunakan di depan subjek yang melakukan suatu perbuatan. Dalam konteks ini, kata by bisa diartikan sebagai oleh atau dengan. My hair was braided by my mother this morning.Menunjukkan cara. Penggunaan kata by yang selanjutnya adalah untuk menunjukkan cara atau metode dalam melakukan sesuatu. Misalnya: It will take about 12 hours, and we need to go by boat.Menunjukkan tujuan. Penggunaan kata by yang terakhir adalah untuk menunjukkan suatu tujuan dari tindakan yang akan atau sedang dilakukan. Misalnya: He is trying to do it well by treating me kindly.

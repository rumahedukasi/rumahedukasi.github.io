If I were you, I'd just go for dessert.


It uses -if conditional sentence type 2 . It states that the speakers can't actualize his/her wish in the present and future.

The pattern should be like this:

If +Subject 1+ verb 2 , subject 2 + would + verb 1


Note :

Auxiliary verb of "were" is used for both subjects, singular and plural.

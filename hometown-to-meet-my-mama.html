26. 1. John came to me yesterday morning. 2. I wrote a letter to my uncle a week ago. 3. He bought some books yesterday. 4. She visited her hometown last year. 5. They wanted to meet at that time.


ྀ༘ Past Tense ྀ༘

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. John came to me yesterday morning.

(–) John didn't come to me yesterday morning(?) Did John come to me yesterday morning?

2. I wrote a letter to my uncle a week ago.

(–) I didn't write a letter to my uncle a week ago. (?) Did I write a letter to my uncle a week ago?

3. He bought some books yesterday.

(–) He didn't buy some books yesterday. (?) Did he buy some books yesterday?

4. She visited her hometown last year.

(–) She didn't visit her hometown last year. (?) Did he visit her hometown last year?

5. They wanted to meet at that time.

(–) They didn't want to meet at that time. (?) Did they want to meet at that time?

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PEMBAHASANSimple Past Tense

▓ Definisi ▓

Simple Past Tense adalah bentuk waktu yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan suatu aktifitas/peristiwa yang dilakukan di masa lampau. Contoh:

Aku belajar bahasa Inggris kemarin (I studied English yesterday) Aku membuat bubur ayam tadi pagi (I made chicken porridge this morning)

▓ Pattern ▓

(+) S + V2 + O/C(–) S + did + not + V2 + O/C(?) Did + S + V2 + O/C+ ?

My hometown is Tasikmalaya, it can be reach about two hours from Bandung. Tasikmalaya is a city and regency in southeastern West Java, Indonesia, between Bandung and Purwokerto on the southerly of two major road routes across Java.
The area is located in the mountainous Preanger region of Java. The population of the entire regency is about 1,58 million. Like most of West Java, it is mostly populated by Muslim, ethnically Sundanese people, with a small Chinese Indonesian minority. The city is sometimes called the “City of a Thousand Pesantren”. For it’s many Islamic religious schools.
The area is known for producing silk goods printed with Batik paper umbrellas, and handbags woven by hand from bamboo and pandanus leaves. The production of handicrafts for domestic and international consumption is an important local industry, in 1998 and 1999, export of handicrafts earned 2.6 billion rupiah for the region.

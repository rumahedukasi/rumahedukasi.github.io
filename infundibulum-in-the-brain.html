Brain cancer might be categorized as the worst and the deadliest disease. Approximately 17.000 people in the United States are diagnosed with primary cancer each year and nearly 13.000 die of the disease. The brain is made up of individual cells. These cells are the smallest units which compose the brain. A brain cancer can arise from any of the cells, which make up the brain. Brain cancer starts in just one cell. As the cell makes more and more copies of itself, it grows to form a tumor. A malignant tumor has a capacity to spread, and is then called “brain cancer.” “Primary brain cancer” starts within the brain, and is the main subject of the transcript. In contrast, “secondary brain cancer” starts in some other organs (like lung or breast) and then spread to the brain. This is called “brain metafisis”. There are several things that have been found to increase the risk of developing brain cancer. Having rare family disease (such as neurofibromatosis, Von-Hippel Lindau), exposure to radiation, and exposure to certain chemicals (like pvc) have been connected to developing brain cancer. 17. bagaimana struktur infundibulum


Infundibulum adalah bagian dari salah satu organ reproduksi wanita yaitu tuba fallopi.

Infundibulum terletak pada bagian ujung tuba dan mempunyai struktur seperti jari-jari yang disebut dengan fimbriae. Fimbria ini berperan dalam aktivitas menangkap, menyapu serta mendorong sel telur/ovum yang telah matang yang dihasilkan oleh ovararium.

PEMBAHASAN LEBIH LANJUT:

Tuba fallopi adalah organ reproduksi wanita yang berfungsi sebagai saluran tempat bertemunya sel telur dan sperma sehingga terbentuk zigot yang tertanam pada uterus.

Organ ini mempunyai 4 bagian yang salah satunya sudah dijelaskan diatas.
Tiga yang lainnya yaitu:
1. Kornu
merupakan bagian perbatasan antara dinding otot uterus dan tuba fallopii yang berperan dalam menjamin hubungan yang stabil dan kuat.

2. Ismus
Bagian yang panjang, sempit, dan menyerupai pensil yang berperan dalam menyeleksi sperma.

3. Ampula
merupakan tempat terjadinya fertilisasi/pembuahan (bertemunya sperma dan ovum).

Organ reproduksi wanita lainnya seperti ovarium/indung telur, uterus/rahim, serviks/mulit rahim dan vagina.

39. 2 is the scanner the "brain" of the machine?............


Jawaban:

No, the scanner is not considered the "brain" of the machine. The brain of a machine refers to its central processing unit (CPU) or main processor, which performs calculations, executes instructions, and controls the overall functioning of the machine. The scanner, on the other hand, is a hardware device that captures and digitizes physical documents or images into a digital format. While the scanner is an important component in certain machines, such as multifunction printers or document management systems, it does not have the same level of processing capabilities as the machine's CPU.

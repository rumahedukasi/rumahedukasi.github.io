36. Bedanya 'carries' , 'is carrying' , 'carried' , dan 'has carried'?


semuanya dari kata Carry yang artinya bawa ._.

Carries : artinya  membawa
(ex. He carries a large bag)

is carrying : artinya sedang membawa
(ex. Luna is carrying that large bag)

Carried : artinya dibawa
(ex. That large bag is carried by Selena)

Has carried : artinya telah dibawa
(ex. He has carried that large bag for 5 days straight)

tapi gatau ketang saya mah gak ahli grammar jadi correct me if i'm wrong ;D

2. what is the difference between commercial films and non-commercial films?​


Jawaban:

Commercial films are all forms of films that are worthy of being shown to the general public without age restrictions, commercial films are usually also in accordance with film supervision standards.while non-commercial films are films that have freedom of expression both visually, intrinsically, and in manuscript, so that most films of this type are not worthy of being shown publicly.

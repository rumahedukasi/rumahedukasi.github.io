Jawaban:

1. I hear beautiful Adzan from mosque. Beautiful is adjective

2. Sinta celebrated her birthday party in Kolong Café last night. Sinta is noun

3. We will spend our holiday in Kawah Ijen next month. Next month is adverb

4. They decorate living room with fresh flower. They is pronoun

5. Wow! That’s wonderful. Wow! Is interjection

6. Mira and her friends enjoy their vacation in Mount Bromo. Their is pronoun

7. My father goes to barber shop today with my uncle. Barber shop is noun

8. Wasiatus takes her doctoral degree in Japan. Takes is verb

9. STIA is amazing campus. Amazing is adjective

10. Mr. Hendrik announces that student must stay at home. Announces is verb

Penjelasan:

→Ada delapan jenis bagian utama part of speech (kelas kata) dalam bahasa Inggris, yaitu nouns, pronouns, adjectives, verbs, adverbs, prepositions, conjunctions dan interjections.

→Nouns (kata benda): Daniel, Sinta, London, table, dog, teacher, pen, city, happiness, hope

→Pronouns (kata ganti): I, you, we, they, he, she, it, me, us, them, him, her, this, those, their

→Adjectives (kata sifat): beautiful, amazing, big, happy, green, young, fun, crazy,

→Verbs (kata kerja): go, speak, run, eat, play, live, walk, have, like, are, is, announce, take

→Adverbs (kata keterangan): slowly, quietly, very, always, never, too, well, tomorrow, here, yesterday, last night

→Prepositions (kata depan): at, on, in, from, with, near, between, about, under

→Conjunctions (kata hubung): and, or, but, because, so, yet, unless, since, if

→Interjections (kata seru): Ouch! Wow! Great! Help! Oh! Hey! Hi!

Semoga bermanfaat

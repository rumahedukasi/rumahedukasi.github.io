12. a carousel with the diameter 3m and moment of inertia 1200 kg.m2 rotates at a speed of 0,8 rad / s.two children weighing 25 kg each jump on the rotating carousel. The speed of the carousel now is.........rad/s?


I1 . ω1 = I2 . ω2
1200 . 0,8 = I2 . ω2
960 = m2 . r² . ω2
960 = 25 . (3/2)² . ω2
960 = 25 . 9/4 . ω2
ω2 = 960 . 4/225
ω2 = 256/15
ω2 = 17,1 rad/s

The speed of the carousel now is 17,1 rad/s

1. analysis on figurative language ini this song lyric do you ever feel like a plastic bag drifting through the wind feel so paper thin like a house of cards


do you ever feel like a plastic bag drifting through the wind
= someone ask you that did you could easily get carried away in any problem or something that you working on

feel so paper thin like a house of cards
= you feel that your feeling (maybe heart) is so fragile that if someone break your heart, you would get down and in deep sorrow

2. Analysis on figurative language on this song lyric( identifikasi majas) do you ever feel like a plastic bag drifting through the wind feel so paper thin like a house of cards


It's a Alegori. Alegory is the main places by other means, through figurative or depictions. Usually by natural means or to make things through nature's character or what is in nature. 
for example, the first and second sentence is describe the feeling like tossed just like a plastic bag. the third and fourth describe the feeling of being weak like a thin paper or a house of card which blow away by a single wind.

[tex]\large\text{$\begin{aligned}&(4-n)!=\frac{(5-n)(4-n)!}{5}+\frac{(6-n)(5-n)(4-n)!}{30}\\\\&....\textsf{ bagi kedua ruas dengan $(4-n)!$}\\\\&1=\frac{(5-n)}{5}+\frac{(6-n)(5-n)}{30}\\\\&1=\frac{6(5-n)+(6-n)(5-n)}{30}\\\\&30=6(5-n)+(6-n)(5-n)\\\\&30=30-6n+30-11n+n^2\\\\&30=60-17n+n^2\\\\&n^2-17n+60-30=0\\\\&n^2-17n+30=0\\\\&(n-2)(n-15)=0\\\\&\textsf{$n=2$ atau $n=15$}\end{aligned}$}[/tex]

The Question!

Paul had some marbles. He gave 20 to Yongjun and ¼ of the rest to Fauzi. He then had ½ of the number of marbles he had at first. How many marbles did Paul have at first?

Penyelesaian!

Diketahui:

» He gave 20 marbles to Yongjun

» And gave ¼ of the rest to Fauzi

» He still had ½ of the number of marbles

Ditanya:

» So, how many marbles did Paul have at first?

----------------------------------------Answer:

½m + 20 + (¼ × (m - 20)) = m

½m + 20 + ¼m - 5 = m

ㅤㅤ ½m + ¼m - m = -20 + 5

²/₄m + ¼m - ⁴/₄m = -15

ㅤㅤㅤ ㅤㅤㅤ-¼m = -15

ㅤㅤㅤㅤ ㅤㅤㅤm = -15 × (-4)

ㅤㅤㅤㅤ ㅤㅤㅤm = 60 ✔

So, the number of marbles that he had first is 60 marbles ✔

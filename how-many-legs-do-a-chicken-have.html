3. How many toes do we have on our both legs?​


JAWABAN :

Ten (10)

PENJELASAN :

'Toes' dalam bahasa indonesia adalah 'Jari kaki'. Jadi, 'How many toes do we have on our both legs' artinya 'Berapa banyak jari kaki yang kita punya di kedua kaki kita'.

Ditanyanya : Berapa banyak jari kaki

Jawaban : Ten

'Ten' dalam bahasa indonesia adalah 'Sepuluh'. Satu kaki kita mempunyai 5 jari, satu lagi juga 5 jari. Jadi, jika digabungkan : 5 + 5 = 10

Jadinya 10 jari. Jadi, jawabannya adalah 'Ten' (10)

11. a farmer owns a total of 20 ducks and and lambs. the ducks and lambs have 64 legs altogether. how many labms are they?​


Jawab: 12 lambs

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

If duck = d and lamb = l, then :

d + l = 20

d = 20 - l

We know that duck has 2 leg and lamb has 4 leg, so we can state that :

2d + 4l = 64

d + 2l = 32

20 - l + 2l = 32

l = 32 - 20

l = 12

It can be concluded that there are 12 lambs.

18. A farmer owns a total of 23 chickens and cows. The chickens and cows have 78 legs altogether. How many cows are there?


Jawaban:

Answer+Explain:

Diket.:

- Sapi + Ayam = 23

- Jumlah kaki sapi + ayam = 78

Misalkan:

Ayam = 2X

Sapi = 4Y

Sehingga:

X + Y = 23

2X + 4Y = 78

X = 23 - Y

2(23 - Y) + 4(Y) = 78

46 -2Y + 4Y = 78

46 + 2Y = 78

2Y = 32

Y = 16

Jumlah sapi = 16

Jumlah ayam = 23 - 16 = 7

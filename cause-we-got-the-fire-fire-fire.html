3. They should have got out of their house when the fire was spreading to their neighbor's house. From the previous sentence we may conclude that ... A. they got trapped in the fire B. they succeeded in escaping from the fire. C. they got out of their house ontime D. the fire did not reach the house E. their neighbor's house was not on fire


They should have got out of their house when the fire was spreading to their neighbor's house. From the previous sentence we may conclude that they got trapped in the fire (A).

Pembahasan

Penggunaan kata 'should have + past participle' untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian yang terjadi di waktu lampau yang seharusnya dilakukan, tetapi pada kenyataannya tidak dilakukan.

Jadi, lebih tepatnya, kalimat saran untuk melakukan sesuatu di waktu lampau yang seharusnya dilakukan.

Bila diartikan: Mereka seharusnya keluar dari rumah mereka ketika api menyebar di rumah tetangganya.

Dari kalimat tersebut, dapat kita simpulan "mereka terjebak di dalam kebaran karena mereka tidak keluar dari rumah ketika kebakaran menyebar di rumah tetangga mereka".

16. The fire...(occur) while we....(be) out simple past tense


The fire occured while we were out

|Past tense|

Occur--> occured

Be --> were



Correct me if iam wrong~

Jawab :

The fire occured while we were out.


Pembahasan :

Simple past digunakan untuk mengambarkan kejadian dimasa lampau. Pattern :


Verbal :

(+) S + V2

(-) S + did not + V1

(?) Did + S + V1?


Non-verbal/Nominal :

(+) S + was/were + O

(-) S + was/were + not + O

(?) Was/were + S + O?


Note :

•-• was digunakan jika subjek berupa I, she, he, it (singular)

•-• were digunakan jika subjek berupa you, they, we, it (plural).

30. ( + ) the ratings extinguished the fire ( - )( ? )​


Jawaban:( + ) the ratings extinguished the fire ( - ) the ratings did not extinguish the fire ( ? ) Did the ratings extinguish the fire?

PEMBAHASAN

Kalimat Positive (+) pada soal menggunakan Verb-2 “extinguished” setelah subject “the ratings”

maka kalimat tersebut Past Tense.

PAST TENSE adalah tenses untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yg terjadi di masa lalu. Rumusnya:

(+) S + V2 + Complement (-) S + did not + V1 + complement(?) Did + S + V1 + complement?

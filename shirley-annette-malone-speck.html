I dreamed it all ever since I was young

Worked so hard, forgot how to vacation

Pembahasan

Simple Past tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang terjadi dan berakhir di masa lampau. Keterangan waktu yang sering digunakan pada simple past tense adalah:

1. Yesterday

2. Last night

3. Last week

4. Last month

5. Last year

6. Three days ago

7. Five weeks ago

8. two months ago

9. four years ago

10. this morning

11. this afternoon

12. dan lain lain

Pattern yang digunakan pada simple past tense adalah:

(+) S + V2

(-) S + did not + V1

(?) Did + S + V1?

Formula present perfect =

subject + has/have + past participle.

Penjelasan :

Has digunakan untuk singular

Have digunakan untuk plural

Shirley -> singular

Jadi yg digunakan has.

Past pasticiple-nya suggest = suggested

Subject + has/have + been + verb + ing.

Penjelasan :

Shirley -> singular

31. Roy, shirley and tim shared a sum of money between them in the ratio 4:9:11. shirley had $100 more than roy. how much money did tim have?


Jawaban:

Let's assume the total sum of money they shared is $x.

According to the ratio, Roy received 4x/24 of the total sum, Shirley received 9x/24, and Tim received 11x/24.

Shirley had $100 more than Roy, so Roy received 4x/24 - $100.

So, the total amount of money received by Roy and Shirley is 4x/24 - $100 + 9x/24 = 13x/24 - $100.

Since they shared the whole sum of money, we can set the equation:

x = 13x/24 - $100 + 11x/24

24x = 24 * (13x/24 - $100) + 24 * (11x/24)

24x = 13x - $2400 + 11x

24x = 24x - $2400

$2400 = 0

Therefore, Tim had $11x/24 of the total sum, which is $11x/24 = $0.

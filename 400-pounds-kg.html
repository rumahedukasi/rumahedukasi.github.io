8. The average number of pounds of meat that a person consumes a year is 218.4 pounds. Assume that the standard deviation is 25 pounds and the distribution is approximately normal​


Based on the given information, the average number of pounds of meat consumed by a person per year is 218.4 pounds, and the standard deviation is 25 pounds. The distribution is assumed to be approximately normal.

To calculate the probability of a person consuming a specific amount of meat or falling within a certain range, we can use the standard normal distribution.

Let's say we want to find the probability of a person consuming less than 200 pounds of meat per year. We need to convert this value to a z-score using the formula:

z = (x - μ) / σ

where:

- x is the value we want to convert (200 pounds in this case)

- μ is the mean (average) of the distribution (218.4 pounds)

- σ is the standard deviation (25 pounds)

Substituting the values:

z = (200 - 218.4) / 25

z ≈ -0.736

Next, we can use a standard normal distribution table or a statistical calculator to find the probability associated with the z-score of -0.736. The probability will represent the area under the curve to the left of the z-score.

Assuming a standard normal distribution table or calculator, we find that the probability is approximately 0.2296 or 22.96%.

Therefore, the probability that a person consumes less than 200 pounds of meat per year is approximately 22.96%.

38. Q1. The weights of a large shipment of cast iron bollards (used for securing ropes on docks) are random variables with mean 50.25 pounds and standard deviation of 1.1 pounds. What is the probability that a bollard selected from this shipment will weigh more than 47.50 pounds?


Jawab:

0,9148

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Untuk menjawab pertanyaan tersebut, kita dapat menggunakan rumus distribusi normal standar. Pertama, kita harus mengubah nilai yang diinginkan (47,50 pound) menjadi nilai Z dengan menggunakan rumus:

Z = (nilai yang diinginkan - mean) / standar deviasi

Setelah nilai Z dihitung, kita dapat mencari nilai probabilitas dengan menggunakan tabel distribusi normal standar. Nilai probabilitas ini merupakan kemungkinan bahwa sebuah bollard yang dipilih akan beratnya lebih dari 47,50 pound.

Dengan menggunakan rumus di atas,

nilai Z yang diperoleh adalah -1,35.

Menggunakan tabel distribusi normal standar, nilai probabilitas untuk

Z = -1,35 adalah 0,9148.

Jadi, kemungkinan bahwa sebuah bollard yang dipilih akan beratnya lebih dari 47,50 pound adalah sebesar 0,9148.

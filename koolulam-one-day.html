Answer:


In translating phrases or sentences, you need to use the proper words accordingly so that it doesn't change or reduce the meaning. Translation word by word or literally is not recommended as it might confuse the reader of the target language.

Here's the Indonesian translation of 'Trust me one day one will come to your defense' is

'Percayalah padaku suatu hari akan ada seseorang yang akan membelamu.'

The phrase 'come to your defense' is an idiom, so it can't be translated word by word literally. That phrase means 'defend/ protect you'.

25. bentuk sederhana dari xy - 5ay + 4xy - 3ay adalah ... 5xy + 8ay 5xy - 8ay 5ay + 8xy 5ay - 8xy ​


-Aljabar-

xy - 5ay + 4xy - 3ay

= xy + 4xy - 5ay - 3ay

= (1+4)xy + (-5-3)ay

= 5xy - 8ay~ Aljabar

♦ Soal:

Bentuk sederhana dari xy – 5ay + 4xy – 3ay adalah....

♦ Penyelesaian:

xy – 5ay + 4xy – 3ay

= xy + 4xy – 5ay – 3ay

= 1xy + 4xy – 8ay

= [tex]\boxed{\green{\sf{5xy - 8ay}}}[/tex]

♦ Kesimpulan:

Jadi, bentuk sederhananya adalah 5xy – 8ay. (Opsi B)

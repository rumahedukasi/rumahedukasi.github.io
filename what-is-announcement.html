Announcement dalam terjemahan bebas Indonesia adalah pengumuman. Tujuan dari announcement adalah untuk menginformasikan atau menjelaskan sesuatu secara singkat.

Hal-hal yang harus ada dalam suatu announcement adalah:

Pembukaan (opening)Judul event/acara (Title of the event )Kepada siapa announcement tersebut ditujukanSiapa yang membuat announcementDate/time, place (Information) Contact Person

There will be an opening ceremony on Monday, 24 February. All the students must wear a white uniform. Please, come before 8 AM.

For more information contact the students' leader.

Thank you. Student Association.

27. what is announcement


Announcement Text

JAWABAN:

Announcement is a statement (official notification) in oral or written form that contains something for everyone to know. However, not everyone who hears or reads the announcement has an interest in the announcement. Announcements can be heard or read anywhere, for example oral announcements at school or in public places, while written announcements can be read on notice boards, in magazines, and in newspapers.

Terjemahannya agar lebih paham:

Announcement adalah pernyataan (pemberitahuan resmi) dalam bentuk lisan atau tulis yang berisi sesuatu untuk di ketahui oleh semua orang. Namun, tidak semua orang yang mendengar atau membaca pengumuman tersebut berkepentingan atas pengumuman tersebut. Pengumuman dapat kita dengar atau baca dimana saja, misalnya pengumuman lisan disekolah atau di tempat-tempat umum, sedangkan pengumuman tertulis dapat kita baca di papan pengumuman, di majalah, dan di koran.

PEMBAHASAN:

Announcement    

Social Function  (Fungsi sosial)

To share planned/arranged activities/agenda  (Untuk berbagi kegiatan / agenda yang direncanakan / diatur)To inform the reader about information an event, job vacancies, new enrolment, new admissions, and so on.  (Untuk menginformasikan kepada pembaca tentang informasi suatu acara, lowongan pekerjaan, pendaftaran baru, penerimaan baru, dan sebagainya)

Generic Structure Announcement (Struktur Pengumuman)

Title (Headings / Purposes): This is the most important part because it represents the overall contents of the announcer. Even so sometimes it is not clearly stated.  (Title (Headings/Purposes): Ini adalah bagian yang paling penting karena mewakili isi keseluruhan dari pengumuna tersebut. Meski begitu kadang tidak disebutkan dengan jelas)Explanation (Detailed information): A further explanation of the announcement. Usually consists of basic information which includes: type of activity, time, place, and participant. (Explanation (Detailed information): Penjelasan lebih lanjut tentang pengumunan tesebut. Biasanya terdiri dari informasi dasar yang mencakup: jenis kegiatan, waktu, tempat, dan partisipant)

Announcement Parts  (Bagian- bagian Announcement)

TheTitle or type of event (Judul atau jenis kejadian ) Date and time (Tanggal dan waktu) Place  (Tempat )Contact person / address (Orang/alamat yang dapat dihubungi)

Example of Announcement  (Contoh Pengumuman )

School Graduation Photo

Based on the instruction from the ministry for education, all of third year students are required to attend and partake in photo shoot session. The photo itself will be attached to your Graduation Certificate.

It is imperative that students preregister online at www.gradsphoto.com before due date in March 4th, 2020. Students also allowed to make a group of 5 and you will get 5 shots for only 20k.

Chief Assistant for Students Relation

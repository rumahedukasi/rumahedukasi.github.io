Eric was working in his shed in the backyard when he heard a strange voice coming from his backyard.He found an enormous lizard in his backyard.Because he was terrified.The lizard was climbing on the wall of Eric's house.At the end, when the lizard saw Eric, it quickly crawled away into the bush.

PEMBAHASAN

[text]

Eric Holland, aged 80, from Thurgoona, New South Wales, got a surprise this morning. He was working in his shed in the backyard when he heard a strange voice coming from his backyard. He checked it and saw an enormous lizard in his backyard. The reptile was crawling among his plants. It was about 1.5 meter long.

He was terrified, so he slowly stepped back into the shed. He thought it was an alien or something. Then he decided to take a look at the lizard once more. This time, it was climbing on the wall of his house. When the lizard saw Eric, it quickly crawled away into the bush.

_

[questions]

↪Number 1

Apa yang Eric lakukan pagi itu?

Jawaban pada teks:

Eric Holland, aged 80, from Thurgoona, New South Wales, got a surprise this morning. He was working in his shed in the backyard when he heard a strange voice coming from his backyard.

Answer:

➥ Eric was working in his shed in the backyard when he heard a strange voice coming from his backyard.

Eric sedang bekerja di gubuknya saat ia mendengar suara aneh yang muncul dari belakang rumah.

↪Number 2

Apa yang dia temukan?

Jawaban pada teks:

He was working in his shed in the backyard when he heard a strange voice coming from his backyard. He checked it and saw an enormous lizard in his backyard. The reptile was crawling among his plants. It was about 1.5 meter long.

Answer:

➥ He found an enormous lizard in his backyard.

Dia menemukan seekor kadal besar di belakang rumahnya.

↪Number 3

Mengapa Eric balik ke gubuknya?

Jawaban pada teks:

He was terrified, so he slowly stepped back into the shed. He thought it was an alien or something.

Answer:

➥ Because he was terrified.

Karena dia ketakutan.

↪Number 4

Apa yang kadal itu lakukan saat Eric melihatnya kembali?

Jawaban pada teks:

Then he decided to take a look at the lizard once more. This time, it was climbing on the wall of his house. When the lizard saw Eric, it quickly crawled away into the bush.

Answer:

➥ The lizard was climbing on the wall of Eric's house.

Kadal itu memanjat dinding rumah Eric.

↪Number 5

Apa yang terjadi di akhir cerita?

Jawaban pada teks:

Then he decided to take a look at the lizard once more. This time, it was climbing on the wall of his house. When the lizard saw Eric, it quickly crawled away into the bush.

Answer:

➥ At the end, when the lizard saw Eric, it quickly crawled away into the bush.

Di akhir, kadal itu langsung masuk ke semak-semak setelah melihat Eric.

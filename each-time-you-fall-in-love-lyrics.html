17. beda nya fall in love sama fell in love apa ya? contonh penggunaannya?makasi ya sebelumnya ​


Perbedaan dari "fall in love dan fell in love" adalah...

Jawaban :

=> Meskipun kedua nya memiliki arti yang sama yaitu "Jatuh cinta" akan tetapi dalam penggunaannya kalimat tersebut sangat berbeda.

1). Fall in love => Bentuk present tense (masa sekarang)
Rumus : S + V1 (s/es) + O
Contoh : I fall in love with you today. (Saya jatuh cinta dengan mu hari ini)

2). Fell in love => Bentuk past tense (masa lampau)
Rumus : S + V2 + O
Contoh : I fell in love with you yesterday. (Saya jatuh cinta dengan mu kemarin)

Fall => V1
Fell => V2
Fallen => V3
==============================

40. Why is it that every time humans fall in love someone must feel hurt and their feelings are always toyed with? Give reasons​


Explanation:

6 Reasons Why Someone's Heartbreak Is Easier

The following are some reasons why someone can suffer because of love.

1. They Let Fear Torture Their Minds and Hearts

Explanation:

They are afraid of losing their loved ones. They fear being played, made a fool of or left with a broken heart. Fear and insecurity come when there is uncertainty. But if you really love someone, you have to be fearless and ready for all the consequences.

2. Not Knowing Their Loved Ones

Explanation:

Humans are afraid of what they don't know. If people didn't try hard to know their loved ones, their minds would be filled with uncertainty and negativity, causing them to suffer. To love is to know, therefore, if you really love your partner, you have to get to know them more as a person.

3. Not Being Able To Feel The Feelings Of The People They Love

Explanation:

People suffer in love because they have no compassion. They don't feel the true feelings of their loved ones, causing misunderstandings. To be happy in a relationship, you and the person you like are united, not only in mind but also in heart.

4. Envy of Others

Explanation:

People suffer because they are not satisfied with what they have. They don't appreciate their blessings, either in a subtle way or not. They also hurt the people they love by not making them feel important. True love does not make you jealous. So, if you want to stop suffering, be grateful and appreciate what you have right now.

5. Keep Lying

Explanation:

Lies rob people of freedom, liars always feel guilty and they often can't sleep at night. The longer the lie is hidden, the more it hurts. So if you don't want to suffer anymore, let the truth set you free.

6. Not Trusting Loved Ones

Explanation:

Without trusting their partner, their minds will not be at ease. Without trusting yourself, you will not gain confidence. Trust is based on evidence. So, if you have no proof that your partner is cheating, don't be paranoid but still believe in him. To believe in yourself and have self-confidence, work on yourself to become a better person.

If you expect too much, you will most likely be disappointed. If you expect too little, you will probably get less. Therefore, don't expect too much of love in return so that you don't suffer frustration, but work harder in giving love so that you will produce more results. Remember that the love you give is for you to keep for life, or even forever.

25. what is the different between subject pronoun and object pronoun?please answer :) thanks :)


Subject pronouns are pronouns that replace the subject in the sentence.
Example: “The boy traveled with friends.” In this sentence, "the boy" is performing the action - the subject is "the boy". He.
Subject: I, you, they, we, he, she
In our above example, “The boy traveled with friends.” The object is "friends". Them.
Object: me, you, them, us, him, her.

Object B:

1. Friction force: Similar to object A, the friction force opposing the motion of object B is given by F_friction_B = μ × N.

30. Object A and object B are in the oil with a density of 800 kg/m³. The hydrostatic pressure obtained by object A is 10,000 N/m² and object B is 8,000 N/m². If the acceleration due to gravity is 10 m/s², find the difference between the depths of object A and object B​


Penjelasan:

given :

the density of oil, rho = 800kg/m³

Pa = 10,000 N/m²

Pb = 8,000 N/m²

g = 10 m/s²

find:

the difference between the depths of object A and object B , => ∆h =?

the solution :

∆P = rho. g. ∆h

(Pa - Pb) = rho. g. ∆h

(10,000 - 8,000) = 800.(10).∆h

2,000 = 8,000 . ∆h

therefore:

∆h = 2000/8000

= 0,25 m

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

First, I am not expert in math but this is how I got that...

Ethan blows 15 balloons in 20 mnts, E= 15/20

Wei blows 18 balloons in 25 mnts, W=18/25

Vishal blows 16 balloons in 21 mnts, V=16/21

E : W : V = 15/20 : 18/25 : 16:21

= 0,75 : 0,72 : 0,76

Thus, Vishal blows faster than the other.

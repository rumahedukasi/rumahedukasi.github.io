3. LIVING THINGS AND NON LIVING THINGS ​


Jawaban:

Living things:

1. Tree (Pohon)

Singular (Tunggal): Tree

Plural (Jamak): Trees

Sentence (Kalimat):

I plant a tree I saw trees

2. Cow (Sapi)

Singular (Tunggal): Cow

Plural (Jamak): Cows

Sentence (Kalimat):

Theres a cowCows makes a milk

3. Flower (Bunga)

Singular (Tunggal): Flower

Plural (Jamak): Flowers

Sentence (Kalimat):

I have a nice flower The flowers are beautiful

4. Horse (Kuda)

Singular (Tunggal): Horse

Plular (Jamak): Horses

Sentence (Kalimat):

I can ride a horseThe horse is so cute

5. Bird (Burung)

Singular (Tunggal): Bird

Plural (Jamak): Birds

Sentence (Kalimat):

Im going to bird watchingThe birds are beautiful

Non living things:

1. Spoon (Sendok)

Singular (Tunggal): Spoon

Plural (Jamak): Spoons

Sentence (Kalimat):

I eat with a spoonThere are a lots of spoon, go get some

2. Fork (Garpu)

Singular (Tunggal): Fork

Plural (Jamak): Forks

Sentence (Kalimat):

You can borrow my forkEmma have a lots of forks

3. Glass (Gelas)

Singular (Tunggal): Glass

Plural (Jamak): Glasses

Sentence (Kalimat):

I drink with a glassI have glasses to borrow with

4. Pencil (Pensil)

Singular (Tunggal): Pencil

Plural (Jamak): Pencils

Sentence (Kalimat):

May i borrow your pencil?I have many pencils

5. Eraser (Penghapus)

Singular (Tunggal): Eraser

Plural (Jamak): Erasers

Sentence (Kalimat):

Nezuko have an eraserThe store sell a lots of eraser

Apapun itu yang berbentuk jamak, tambahkan "s" di belakang object seperti: car = car(s), ada pun glass (gelas/kaca) ditambahkan dengan "es" seperti glass = glass(es). Mohon maaf kalau salah :) semoga membantu! :D (Kalau tidak mengerti, silahkan komen aja ^^).

19. Joni ..... in Bandung for five years before he moved in Semarang. A. have been living B. has been living C. had been living D. living E. live


Past perfect continous
jawaban : c. had been livingJoni Has been living in Bandung For five Years before he moved in Semarang.

Jawaban B.

Penjelasan :

Joni Adalah Subjek Laki-laki yang dalam bahasa inggris termasuk "He"
Ini merupakan Perfect Tenses. dan Ketika Subjeknya adalah "he" maka Harus menggunakan "Has" sebagai tobe nya. 

Rumus untuk Kalimat ini (Perfect Tenses) :

Subjek + HAVE(gunakan "HAVE" jika subjek i,you,we,they) / HAS(gunakan "HAS" jika subjek He,She,It) + Past Participle / Verb 3

11. Sinta … ( write) a letter a. am writing c. are writing b. were writing d. is writing


~ Answer:D.is writing

Sinta is writing a letter.

.~ Introduction:

Present continuous tense merupakan kalimat yang menyatakan suatu pekerjaan sedang berlangsung atau sedang berlangsung pada waktu masa kini.

Untuk menyatakan present continuous tense:

pastikan kalimat dilengkapi dengan to be (is/am/are) untuk present tensenyatakan verba dalam bentuk gerund yaitu menambahkan sufiks -ing terhadap verbanya (kata kerja)

~ Discussion:

Sinta … ( write) a letter.

untuk melengkapi kalimat di atas dengan memahami persetujuan pemilihan kata kerja bantu menurut subyek pada kalimatS + is/am/are + V-ing + ...

dengan is/am/are dipilih salah satu menurut subyeknya.

is: he, she, it, name, objectam: Iare: you, they, we, names, objects

Dengan subyek Sinta merupakan person's name, disebutkan hanya satu, menggunakan is dan write menjadi writing.

--> jawabannya:

15. (+) I am writing the letter now(-) (?) (-?)​


Soal:

(+) I am writing the letter now

(-)

(?)

(-?)​

Jawaban:

(-) I AM NOT WRITING the letter now

(?) AM I WRITING the letter now?

(-?) AM I NOT WRITING the letter now?

= Yes, I AM

= No, I AM NOT

Penjelasan:

Pada kalimat Positive bentuk PRESENT CONTINUES diketahui melalui keterangan NOW

(+) S + is/am/are + VERB+ing + O

= I + AM + WRITING + the letter now

(-) S + is/am/are + NOT + VERB+ing + O

= I + AM + NOT + WRITING + the letter now

(?) is/am/are + S + VERB+ing + O

= AM + I + WRITING + the letter now?

(-?) is/am/are + S + NOT + VERB+ing + O

= AM + I + NOT + WRITING + the letter now

✅ YES, S + To Be

= Yes, I + AM

❎ NO, S + To Be + Not

= No, I + AM + Not.

Jawaban:

How to Write a Formal Letter?

1 Address or greet the concerned person properly like Dear Sir/Madam.

2 Always mention the subject of writing the letter.

3 Be concise in your letter. ...

4 The tone of the letter should be very polite and not harsh.

5 Write in a proper format and take care of the presentation of a letter.

6 Mention the address and date correctly.

Jawaban:

• Address or greet the concerned person properly like Dear Sir/Madam.

• Always mention the subject of writing the letter

• Be concise in your letter. Write the reason for writing the letter in the first paragraph itself. Do not stretch the letter too much.

• The tone of the letter should be very polite and not harsh

• Write in a proper format and take care of the presentation of a letter

• Mention the address and date correctly.

• Mention the name and designation of the recipient correctly

The closing of the letter should be with gratitude. Use • •“Thank you” for consideration of the letter and then at last mention “Yours sincerely or truly” along with your name and signature.

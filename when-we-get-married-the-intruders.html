33. kalau di narrative text itu pakainya "got married" atau "get married" ya??​

Jawaban:

The text is about an invitation to a couples shower honoring.John Sample and Jane Sample who are going to get married.The party will be held in February 14th at 4 pm.The party is located in the Tap Room 123, Wed Dr. Phila. Pa 55555If we can or can't come to the party, we can inform them through text message or phone call.The communicative purpose of the text is to invite the readers to join their couples shower honoring.

Note:

Couples shower honoring atau bridal shower merupakan pesta untuk merayakan berakhirnya masa lajang (single) seorang perempuan atau laki-laki. Fungsinya adalah untuk memberikan ucapan selamat, bersenang-senang, dan menghabiskan waktu bersama sebelum calon pengantin resmi menikah.

4. Jelaskan pengertian local broadcast dan directed broadcast!


Local(255.255.255.255) means that a router will not forward the packet (in some cases like DHCP the router will convert it to a unicast packet and forward it). Directed broadcast addresses have just the host part of the address as all 1s and routers will forward so if I have a subnet 10.10.10.0/24 I can ping from elsewhere in the network 10.10.10.255 and I would get an answer from all hosts. However there are a number of attack that exploit and you can configure your routers to not forward directed broadcasts.

Rumus Passive Voice:

1. Passive Voice [Simple Present Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Verb3

2. Passive Voice [Present Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + being + Verb3

3. Passive Voice [Present Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + have/has + been + Verb3

4. Passive Voice [Simple Past Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Verb 3

5. Passive Voice [Past Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Being + Verb3

6. Passive Voice [Past Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + had + been + Verb3

7. Passive Voice [Simple Future Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will + be + Verb3

8. Passive Voice [Future Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will be + being + Verb3

9. Passive Voice [Future Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will have + been + Verb3

Adjective Phrase - Reduce Adjective Clause

1.      E.T., directed by Steven Spielberg, was the top-earning film until it was passed by Titanic and the reissued Star Wars.    

2.      Spider-Man, based on the popular comic book, is the fifth-highest-earning movie of all time.

3.      The Matrix and The Matrix: Reloaded, starring Keanu Reeves, are both very popular.  

4.      James Cameron has directed many big movies including Titanic, True Lies, and the Terminator films.    

5.      The Harry Potter novels, written by J. K. Rowling, have translated well to the screen.  

6.      Star Wars, The Empire Strikes Back, and Return of the Jedi, featuring Harrison Ford, Carrie Fisher, and Mark Hammill, were conceived, written, and directed by George Lucas.

ーーーーーーーーーーーー

Explication

Adjective phrase adalah frasa yang berfungsi sebagai penjelas kata/frasa benda. Suatu adjective phrase merupakan pemadatan dari sebuah adjective clause.

Contoh:

Sentence with adjective clause:

The house which is built in 1950 will be torn down.

Adjective clause → which is built in 1950

Adjective phrase → built in 1950

Sentence with adjective phrase:

The house built in 1950 will be torn down.

Cara memadatkan adjective clause menjadi adjective phrase:

hilangkan relative pronoun + be (is/am/are/was/were) jika adaJika tidak ada be, ubah kata kerja menjadi bentuk present participle (v-ing).

Contoh:

E.T. was directed by Steven Spielberg. It was the top-earning film until it was passed by Titanic and the reissued Star Wars.

Sentence with adjective clause:

E.T., which was directed by Steven Spielberg, was the top-earning film until it was passed by Titanic and the reissued Star Wars.

Sentence with adjective phrase:

E.T., directed by Steven Spielberg, was the top-earning film until it was passed by Titanic and the reissued Star Wars.

The Matrix and The Matrix: Reloaded star Keanu Reeves. They are both very popular.

Sentence with adjective clause:

The Matrix and The Matrix: Reloaded, which star Keanu Reeves, are both very popular.

Sentence with adjective phrase:

The Matrix and The Matrix: Reloaded, starring Keanu Reeves, are both very popular.

39. Jelaskan hakikat guru profesional sebagai seorang pembelajar yang mandiri (teacher as self- directed learner), yang membangun pengetahuan praktik profesionalnya untuk meningkatkan kualitas pembelajaran secara bermakna.


Jawaban:

Hakikat guru profesional sebagai seorang pembelajar yang mandiri, atau teacher as self-directed learner, adalah pemahaman bahwa seorang guru secara aktif terlibat dalam pengembangan pengetahuan dan keterampilan mereka sendiri untuk meningkatkan praktik pengajaran mereka. Sebagai pembelajar yang mandiri, seorang guru tidak hanya mengandalkan pelatihan awal atau pengalaman masa lalu, tetapi secara berkelanjutan mencari peluang untuk belajar, mengembangkan diri, dan meningkatkan kualitas pembelajaran yang mereka tawarkan kepada siswa.

Berikut adalah beberapa aspek yang melibatkan hakikat guru profesional sebagai seorang pembelajar yang mandiri:

1. Refleksi dan Analisis Diri:

Guru sebagai pembelajar yang mandiri terlibat dalam proses refleksi dan analisis diri terhadap praktik pengajaran mereka. Mereka secara aktif merefleksikan pengalaman mereka di kelas, mengevaluasi efektivitas metode pengajaran yang mereka gunakan, dan mencari cara untuk meningkatkan pendekatan mereka.

2. Penelitian dan Pembaruan Pengetahuan:

Guru yang mandiri sebagai pembelajar terus mengembangkan pengetahuan mereka dengan menjaga diri mereka up-to-date dengan perkembangan terkini di bidang pendidikan. Mereka membaca jurnal akademik, mengikuti seminar dan konferensi, menjalin hubungan dengan komunitas pembelajaran profesional, dan terus mencari informasi baru yang relevan dengan praktik pengajaran mereka.

3. Kolaborasi dan Berbagi:

Guru yang mandiri sebagai pembelajar tidak hanya mencari pengetahuan untuk diri mereka sendiri, tetapi juga berbagi pengetahuan dan pengalaman mereka dengan sesama guru.

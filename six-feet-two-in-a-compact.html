39. The school year in Japan from April to March. Summer vacation last about six weeks. In addition to national holidays, school children also have two weeks off at New Year and two weeks break in the spring, between the old and new school year. How many weeks do students have holidays in a school year in Japan? Pilih salah satu: a. six b. eight c. ten d. twelve e. two


Reading Comprehension

Below there is a question related to reading text of “the school year in Japan”, here it is:

The school year in Japan from April to March. Summer vacation last about six weeks. In addition to national holidays, school children also have two weeks off at New Year and two weeks break in the spring, between the old and new school year. How many weeks do students have holidays in a school year in Japan?

The answer is C. Ten

Pembahasan:

The students have holidays in a school year in Japan is 10 weeks. Why is ten? Because this answer is concluded based on calculations from six weeks of summer vacation plus two weeks off at New Year plus two weeks break in Spring. So, all total the student’s holiday including national holidays is 22 weeks.

Explanation

Summer Vacation: 6 weeksSpring break : 2 weeksNew Year off: 2 weeksNational Holidays: twice per week or 1 week in a month or 12 weeks in a year

Calculation

6 weeks + 12 weeks + 2 weeks + 2 weeks = 22 weeks

So, total total the students holiday are 22 weeks.

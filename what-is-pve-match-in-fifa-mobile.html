33. E. Discuss. What would life be like without mobile phones? What would you like mobile phones to be able to do in the future? @​


Jawaban:

Life without mobile phones would be significantly different. Mobile phones have become an integral part of our daily lives, serving as communication devices, entertainment platforms, and tools for various tasks. Without mobile phones, we would rely more on landline phones and physical interactions for communication. Accessing information and services on the go would be more challenging, as mobile internet and apps have become essential for tasks like navigation, online shopping, and banking.

As for the future of mobile phones, there are several advancements that could be exciting. Some possibilities include:

1. Enhanced battery life: Longer battery life would allow for extended usage without frequent recharging.

2. Augmented reality (AR): Integration of advanced AR capabilities would enable immersive experiences and practical applications in fields like education, gaming, and productivity.

3. Improved security: Stronger security measures, such as advanced biometrics or encryption technologies, could safeguard personal data and enhance user privacy.

4. Health monitoring: Mobile phones with built-in health sensors and AI capabilities could track vital signs, provide health insights, and offer personalized recommendations for wellness and fitness.

5. Sustainable design: Development of eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient technologies could make mobile phones more environmentally friendly.

These are just a few possibilities, and the future of mobile phones holds great potential for further integration of technology into our lives, making them even more versatile, convenient, and impactful.

39. who is the winner in the badminton match​


Pemain yang memenangkan 2 set (atau game) akan menjadi pemenang pertandingan. Namun, jika seorang pemain memenangkan 2 set pertama, ia akan dinyatakan sebagai pemenang pertandingan. Set ketiga tidak perlu dimainkan karena pemain telah memenangkan 2 pertandingan langsung.

Jawaban:

The player(s) who wins 2 sets (or games) will be the winner of the match. However if a player(s) wins the first 2 sets, he/she will be declared the winner of the match. The third set is not needed to be played since the player has already won 2 straight games.

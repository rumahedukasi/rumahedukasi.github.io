6. Tolong buatkan dialog yang ada imperative,invite,asking for permission untuk 4 orang


CREATE A DIALOGUE USING IMPERATIVE, INVITE AND ASKING PERMISSION JawabanPendahuluan

Untuk membuat dialog kita perlu menetapkan tema, situasi dan jumlah orang dalam dialog, dalam soal ini diminta 4 orang. Selanjutnya kita perlu memahami ekpresi yang akan digunakan dalam pembuatan dialog, yaitu Imperative, invite dan asking permission.

EXPRESSION IMPERATIVE

Expression imperative atau kalimat imperative adalah kalimat untuk memberikan perintah atau instruksi, pada dasarnya kalimat ini menyuruh seseorang untuk melakukan sesuatu. Contoh kalimat imperative :

1. Clean your bedroom!

2. Please be quiet!

3. Wait for me!

4. Close the windows!

5. Be there at three!

6. Help me!

EXPRESSION INVITING

Expression inviting adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk mengundang/mengajak seseorang untuk bergabung atau melakukan sesuatu atau pergi ke suatu tempat. Contoh kalimat invitation :

1. Would you like to have dinner with us?

2. Can you join us?

3. Let's go to the library!

4. Will you come to my party?

5. How about going to the zoo this weekend?

6. Would you like to dance with me/

7. Will you join us for the gathering event?

EXPRESSION ASKING PERMISSION

Expression asking permission adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk menanyakan persetujuan orang lain mengenai hal yang akan dilakukan atau ijin untuk menggunakan barang milik orang lain, seringkali diakhiri dengan kata please, untuk menunjukkan kesopanan. Contoh kalimat :

1. May I use your computer?

2. Can I take a look at your book?

3. Do you mind if i open the window?

4. Can I ask a question?

5. Can you please turn off the TV?

Pembahasan

Situation : In the classroom, the teacher asked the student to be quiet, because Nadia, the class leader has an important announcement about the class gathering. She invited Nadia to the front of the class to give the announcement. After the announcement, some students started ask some questions.

Teacher : Good morning children! Nadia will give an announcement about the class gathering. Please be quiet!. Nadia, will you come to the front and give the announcement?

Nadia : Thanks Teacher. OK, We had have a discussion earlier this week and decided that for the class gathering we are going to the water park next Saturday, 25 April. The meeting point would be here in the classroom at 8.30 am.

Alex  : Can we have the details of the gathering?

Nadia : Farah, Can you answer that question, please?

Farah : The rundown still on progress. It would be ready by noon tomorrow. But I need someone to help me to print and distribute them.

Ardi : I can help with that, just give me the USB tomorrow then I'll print them out.

Nadia : Thanks Ardi.  We also invite all the parents to join us. Teacher, Would you like to join the class gathering?

Teacher : I would love too. Thank you for inviting me.

Nadia : You're welcome, Anyone still have a question?

Ardi : Do we need to bring lunch and picnic mat for the gathering?

Nadia : NO, you don't need to bring lunch but personal water bottle would be good. The parents leader will organize the lunch and snacks for us. If there is no more questions I would like to thank everyone for the support!

Teacher : Thanks Nadia. Now, Open your Math book! We have a math test tomorrow!

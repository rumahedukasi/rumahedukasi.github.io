3. Jess mempunyai 2 buah kubus dengan panjang sisi 18 cm dan 21 cm. Tentukan selisih volume kedua kubus Jess!​


semoga membantu yaaa jawabanya

Jess mempunyai 2 buah kubus dengan panjang sisi 18 cm dan 21 cm. Maka, selisih volume kedua kubus Jess adalah 3.429 cm³

Penjelasan dengan langkah - langkah

Kubus adalah sebuah bangun ruang 3 dimensi dengan 6 sisi datar yang berbentuk persegi dan masing masing panjang sisinya(rusuk) sama panjang. Untuk menyelesaikan masalah soal ini, langkah pertama kita harus menghitung masing-masing volumenya dahulu. Lalu, langkah selanjutnya adalah dengan melakukan pengurangan hasil volume yang lebih besar dengan hasil volume yang lebih kecil.

Rumus volume kubus :

[tex]{ \boxed{ \sf V = s \times s \times s}}[/tex]

Dengan Keterangan :

V = Volumes = Panjang sisiPenyelesaian soal-!

Diketahui:

Panjang sisi kubus pertama = 18 cm

Panjang sisi kubus kedua = 21 cm

Ditanya :

selisih volume kedua kubus = ...

Jawab :

- Langkah pertama, Kita hitung dahulu volumenya masing²

Volume Kubus pertama

= s × s × s

= 18 × 18 × 18

= 324 × 18

= 5.832 cm³

Volume Kubus Kedua

= s × s × s

= 21 × 21 × 21

= 441 × 21

= 9.261 cm³

- Langkah Kedua, kita hitung selisihnya

Selisih volume kedua kubus

= Volume kubus kedua - Volume kubus pertama

= 9.261 cm³ - 5.832 cm³

= [tex]{ \boxed{ \tt 3.429 \: cm³}}[/tex]

Kesimpulan:

Jadi, selisih volume kedua kubus Jess adalah [tex]{ \boxed{ \tt 3.429 \: cm³}}[/tex]

22. Raj and Jess live on the same long road. Jess left her home and rode her bike along the road at a constant speed of 18 km/h. Raj left his home 10 minutes after Jess. He drove his car in the same direction as Jess rode. Raj overtook Jess after driving for 40 minutes at a constant speed of 75 km/h. How many kilometres from Jess does Raj live?Explain the process since i know the answer is 35km. step by step please​


Jawaban:

Let's solve the problem step by step:

1. Convert the given speeds from km/h to km/min, since the time measurements given in the problem are in minutes. To convert km/h to km/min, we divide by 60.

- Jess's speed: 18 km/h ÷ 60 = 0.3 km/min

- Raj's speed: 75 km/h ÷ 60 = 1.25 km/min

2. Convert the given times from minutes to hours, since the speeds are in km/h.

- Raj left his home 10 minutes after Jess. This is 10/60 = 1/6 hours after Jess.

- Raj overtook Jess after driving for 40 minutes. This is 40/60 = 2/3 hours after Raj left his home.

3. Use the formula distance = speed × time to find the distance each person traveled.

- Jess's distance = speed × time = 0.3 km/min × t, where t is the time Jess rode her bike.

- Raj's distance = speed × time = 1.25 km/min × (t - 1/6), since Raj started driving 10 minutes after Jess.

4. Since Raj overtakes Jess, their distances must be equal at that point. Therefore, we can set the two distance formulas equal to each other and solve for t.

- 0.3t = 1.25(t - 1/6) + 40

- 0.3t = 1.25t - 5/24 + 40

- 0.95t = 40 + 5/24

- t = (40 + 5/24) ÷ 0.95

- t ≈ 42.11 minutes

5. Now that we know t, we can find the distance from Jess to Raj.

- Jess's distance = 0.3 km/min × 42.11 min ≈ 12.63 km

- Raj's distance = 1.25 km/min × (42.11 - 1/6) ≈ 48.37 km

- Distance from Jess to Raj = Raj's distance - Jess's distance ≈ 48.37 km - 12.63 km = 35.74 km

Therefore, Raj lives approximately 35.74 km from Jess.

[tex]\lim_{x \to \frac{\pi}{2}} \frac{4(x-\pi)cos^2x}{\pi(\pi-2x)tan(x-\frac{\pi}{2})}\\\\=\lim_{x \to \frac{\pi}{2}} \frac{4(x-\pi)(sin(\frac{\pi}{2}-x))^2}{-2\pi(x-\frac{\pi}{2} )tan(x-\frac{\pi}{2})}\\\\=-2\lim_{x \to \frac{\pi}{2}} \frac{(x-\pi)(-sin(x-\frac{\pi}{2}))^2}{\pi(x-\frac{\pi}{2} )tan(x-\frac{\pi}{2})}\\[/tex]

= x² + 4x + 5

= 2x² + 8x + 10

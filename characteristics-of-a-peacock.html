1. the facts of peacock


1. the male peacock has a colorful feather then the female one because of the male peacock use his beautyful feather to make the female peacock interested with him
2. he life in the rainforest.
3. peacock have a widespread at rainforest near india,africa,asia,and pakistan
4. he eat a berry fruits,insect,and the other small animals
1.Peacocks eat plant parts, flower petals, seed heads, insects and other arthropods, reptiles and amphibians.Peacocks are polygamous by nature.
2.In the wild, male Peacocks generally have a harem of 2 - 5 females.Peacocks have a significant need for companionship.
3.Alone, they can get heartbroken.
4.Only a peacock (male) has a colorful train.
5.The main use of the beautiful train (tail) of peacock is to gain the attention of the peahen and get her to mate with him.
6.The train of a peacock makes up more than 60 percent of his total length.
7.A peacock’s train can be arched into a splendid fan, which reaches across its back and touches the ground on either side.It is believed that a peahen choose its mates according to the size, color, and quality of their trains.
8.Keeping in mind the combined length of a peacock’s (male) train and its large wingspan, it is one of the largest flying birds.Every portion of a peacock’s train changes color when it is struck by different angles of light.
9.The chick of a peacock can walk, eat and drink on its own, even when it is hardly a day old.
10.Peacocks fly into trees to protect themselves from predators as well as to rest from holding their trains off the ground.
11.A family of peacocks is called a bevy, while a group of peacocks is known as a partyAt night, peacocks prefer to roost in trees or other high places.

Indian Peafowl or peacock India, whose scientific name Pavo cristatus is one of three species of birds peacocks. Indian Peafowl has a shiny dark blue feathers. Large-sized adult male birds, to a length of 230cm, with a very long tail cover, metallic green.

Above his head are erect-crested blue to form a fan. Females are smaller than males. The feathers are not shiny, greenish brown with black stripes and without cover with fur tail. Young birds as female.

Common peafowl population scattered in open forests with grasslands in India, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Nepal and Bhutan. Previous species is found also in Bangladesh, but now likely extinct there.

15. Mention characteristics of memo


Characteristic of memo can be seen as follows:

Used within organization/ company (internal use) Focus on one subject The content is short, to the point, and clear. The language feature is less formal than letters but maintains the polite tone. Started by mentioning the purpose and ended by mentioning the action that should be taken by the receiver.

13. buat present continues (+),(-),(?).bayu alway study english at home every day with his friend​


Sebelumnya apakah kalimat yang disajikan diatas sudah betul pola tense yang diminta dan juga struktur kalimatnya? Keterangan waktu ( always dan everyday ) tidak bisa digabungkan dalam satu kalimat harus di pilih salah satunya. Keterangan waktu ( always, everyday, often ) menunjukan pola simple present tense.

Simple Present tense

Menyatakan Fakta ( general truth )Mendeskripsikan kebiasaan ( habitual activity )Menggunakan adverb of time ( always, everyday, often, seldom, rarely, usually )

1. Pola simple present tense bentuk positive (+)

Subject + verb ( s/es ) + Object

Bayu always studies English at home with his friends

Bayu studies English at home with his friends everyday.

penjelasan :

Akhiran -es yang dicetak tebal menunjukkan subyek tunggal ( Budi )

Kata keterangan ( everyday) yang dicetak tebal menunjukkan kebiasaan sehingga sebagai ciri khas dari simple present tense.

2. Pola simple present tense bentuk negatif (-)

Subject+ do/does + not + verb 1 + Object + adverb.

Bayu does not always study English at home with his friends

Bayu does not study English at home with his friends everyday.

penjelasan :

Kalimat negatif memiliki ciri khas ada " not " nya. Karena kalimat verbal ( kalimat yang predikatnya kata kerja aksi ) harus ditambahkan "does" karena subyeknya tunggal.

3. Pola simple present tense bentuk interrogative ( ?)

Do/does+Subject+verb 1 + Object + adverb?

Does Bayu always study English at home with his friends ?

Does Bayu study English at home with his friends everyday?

Penjelasan :

"Does" sebagai auxilary verb harus diletakkan di awal kalimat tanya. "Does" bergantung pada Subyek tunggal nya.

Present Continuous Tense

Menyatakan kejadian yang sedang berlangsung ( dibicarakan )keterengan waktu " Now" , "Right Now"

1. Pola Present Continuous tense bentuk positive ( + )

Subject + tobe ( is/am/are ) + verb-ing + object + Adverb

Bayu is studying English at home with his friend now.

2. Pola Present Continuous tense bentuk negative ( - )

Subject + tobe ( is/am/are ) + verb-ing + object + adverb

Bayu is not studying English at home with his friend  now.

3 Pola Present Continuous tense bentuk interrogative  ( ? )

tobe ( is/am/are )+Subject+verb-ing + object + adverb?

Is Bayu studying English at home with his friend now?

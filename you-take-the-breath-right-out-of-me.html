27. Change the verb in the sentence bellow into past perfect continuous tense! Ronaldo was leaning agains the wall,out of breath. He .... (run)​


He had been running.

Ronaldo was leaning against the wall, out of breath, because he had been running.

Soal: ubah ke bentuk Past Perfect Continues

Jawab: He had been running

= Ronaldo was leaning against the wall,out of breath. He had been running

Catatan:

Rumus Past Perfect Continues

(+) S + had been + V+ing

= He had been running

Materi: Past Perfect Continues

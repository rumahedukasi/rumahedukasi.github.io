6. perbedaan general classification and description?


Report Text

General classification and description are the generic structure of the report text. Here are the explanation:

General Classification is general statement that introduces the topic of the report and its classification. Description tells what the phenomenon under discussion ; in terms of shape/form, parts, qualities, habits or behaviors.

PEMBAHASAN:

Report text is a type of text that presents information from an observation, research, observation, or study of objects, animals, people, or a place. The function or purpose of the report text is to describe the object as it is.  

"Report text adalah suatu jenis teks yang menghadirkan informasi dari suatu hasil pengamatan, penelitian, observasi, atau studi tentang benda, binatang, orang, atau suatu tempat. Fungsi atau tujuan dari report text adalah untuk menggambarkan objek apa adanya".  

Language Features of Report Text

General nouns, maksudnya adalah, suatu benda (baik itu hidup atau mati) yang bersifat umum.  Relating verbs, dalam grammar disebut juga dengan linking verbs. Seperti to be [is, am, are: present], seem, look, taste dan lain sebagainya.  Timeless present tense adalah salah satu penanda waktu dalam simple present seperti "often, usually, always" dan lain-lain.  Technical terms, maksudnya adalah istilah-istilah yang meliputi teks report tersebut. Misalnya tentang "music" maka, istilah-istilah musik harus ada

9. General classification about Library


A library classification is a system by which library resources are arranged according to subject. Library classifications use a notational system that represents the order of topics in the classification and allows items to be stored in that order. Library classification systems group related materials together, typically arranged in a hierarchical tree structure. A different kind of classification system, called a faceted classification system, is also widely used which allows the assignment of multiple classifications to an object, enabling the classifications to be ordered in multiple ways. The library classification numbers can be considered identifiers for resources but are distinct from the International Standard Book Number (ISBN) or International Standard Serial Number (ISSN) system.

11. Report text tentang semut yang menggunakan general classification


Answer:
Report text is a text which contains an information about an event or something.
Report text is used to describe something in general in order to inform the readers.
The generic structure of the report text:
1. General classification
It contains the general description of the subject of the text.
2. Description
It contains about the specific description of the subject such as parts of the body, behavior, and habitat.

17. general classification and description of rabbit


Rabbits are small mammals found naturally in Europe, South Africa, Sumatra and Japan. Rabbits are also often found in the desert regions of the Middle East where the rabbits inhabit the greener parts of the deserts where there is enough food and water for the rabbits to survive. Rabbits are herbivores that feed on mainly on grass, but rabbits also eat nuts and berries and often fruit and vegetables. Rabbits dig burrows into the ground where the rabbit hides and stores food, and also gives birth to and raises the baby rabbits. Today, the rabbit is a popular pet particularly with young children due to the rabbits calm and quiet nature. Rabbits are natures lawnmowers and can eat grass all day long. It is vital that pet rabbits eat enough grass as the grass is not only good for them, but also helps to keep the teeth of the rabbit healthy. Rabbit teeth grow constantly and if the rabbit is not able to gnaw on things to keep them down, then their teeth can grow extremely long which causes pain and often death to the rabbit. The average rabbit lives to about 8 years old when kept as a pet, but many wild rabbits do not live as long due to the fact that the rabbit is prey to many predators including cats, dogs and humans. Rabbits are also prone to myxomatosis, a disease that causes the rabbit to rapidly develop tumors which quickly results in death. Pet rabbits should be vaccinated against the disease every 6 months to a year which prevents the disease from becoming fatal should the rabbit catch it. Rabbits are seen as pests by farmers and gardeners alike due to their destructive nature when they are around lush vegetation. In Australia, the myxomatosis virus was deliberately introduced as a form of pest control for the numerous rabbits that were inhabiting and eating their way through the plant life. Today their are more than 50 different species of rabbit and the number continues to increase as the selective breeding of pet rabbits becomes more popular. The smallest species of domestic rabbit is the mini lop which weighs around 5 lbs, and the largest species of domestic rabbits is the flemish giant rabbit which weighs between 5 kg and 9kg and is the largest species of rabbit in the world. Rabbits are often used as a symbol of fertility or rebirth, and have long been associated with spring and Easter as the Easter Bunny. Rabbits are well known for their quick and successful breeding with the average gestation period of the rabbit being just over a month, with the female rabbit then giving birth to an average of 6 babies.

Bunga matahari adalah tanaman tahunan. Biasanya tumbuh, bunga, dan mati dalam satu tahun. memiliki kepala berbunga besar. Batang bunga matahari bisa tumbuh setinggi 3m, dan kepala bunga bisa mencapai diameter 30 cm. Istilah "bunga matahari" juga mengacu pada semua tanaman genus Helianthus, sejenis tanaman abadi. Artinya mereka bisa tumbuh selama dua musim atau lebih. Untuk tumbuh dengan baik, bunga matahari membutuhkan sinar matahari penuh. Mereka tumbuh paling baik di tanah yang subur, lembab, terkuras dengan baik dengan banyak perlindungan tanah. Dalam penanaman komersial, benih ditanam berdiameter 45 cm dan kedalaman 2,5 cm. Bunga itu asli Amerika. Buktinya adalah pertama kali ditanam di Meksiko, setidaknya pada pukul 2600 SM. Ini mungkin telah ditanam untuk kedua kalinya di tengah Lembah Mississippi, atau diperkenalkan di sana dari Meksiko pada tanggal awal, seperti jagung! Contoh paling awal dari bunga matahari yang ditanam sepenuhnya ditemukan di Tenn dan bertanggal sekitar 2300 SM. Banyak penduduk asli Amerika menggunakan bunga matahari sebagai simbol dewa Matahari mereka, termasuk orang-orang Aztec dan Otomi Meksiko dan suku Inca di Amerika Selatan. Bunga matahari "whole seed" (buah) dijual sebagai makanan ringan, setelah dipanggang di oven, dengan atau tanpa garam ditambahkan. Bunga matahari bisa diolah menjadi alternatif selai kacang, mentega matahari. Di Jerman, bunga matahari dicampur bersama tepung terigu untuk membuat Sonnenblumenkernbrot (secara iterally: roti biji bunga matahari), yang cukup populer di Eropa yang berbahasa Jerman. Ini juga dijual sebagai makanan untuk burung dan bisa langsung digunakan dalam memasak dan salad. Minyak bunga matahari, yang diekstrak dari biji, digunakan untuk memasak. Ini mengandung minyak, sehingga bisa menghasilkan margarin dan biodiesel.

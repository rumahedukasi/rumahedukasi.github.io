4. "Bambang lost his mobile phone, but he was afraid to tell to his parents." ubah kalimat ini jadi kalimat negative dan kalimat interrogative


Soal:

ubah kalimat jadi kalimat negative (-) dan kalimat interrogative (?)

"Bambang lost his mobile phone, but he was afraid to tell to his parents."

Jawab:

(-) Bambang did not lose his mobile phone, but he was not afraid to tell to his parents.

(?) did Bambang lose his mobile phone?

but was he afraid to tell to his parents.?

Pembahasan:

Karena kalimat dalam soal bentuk Past Tense diketahui melalui Verb 2 dan To be Was

1. Verbal Sentence

(+) S + Verb 2 + O/C

= Bambang lost his mobile phone

(-) S + did not + Verb 1 + O/C

= Bambang did not lose his mobile phone

(?) did + S + verb 1 + O/C

= did Bambang lose his mobile phone?

2. Nominal sentence

(+) S + was/were + Adj/adv/noun

= [but] he was afraid to tell to his parents

(-) S + was/were + not + adj/adv/noun

= [but] he was not afraid to tell to his parents.

(?) was/were + S + adj/adv/noun

= [but] was he afraid to tell to his parents.?

32. Angga bought a television at price of Rp8.200.000, after a month he want to change his TV and buy a new one. If he lost Rp520.000 by selling it, how much he sold his TV?​


Jawab:

Rp7.680.000.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Angga bought a television for Rp8.200.000, and then sold it a month later for Rp520.000 less than the purchase price. To find out how much he sold the TV for, we can subtract the amount he lost from the purchase price:

Selling price = Purchase price - Amount lost

Selling price = Rp8.200.000 - Rp520.000

Selling price = Rp7.680.000

Therefore, Angga sold his television for Rp7.680.000.

40. Ubahlah ke kalimat interrogative dan negative :"Bambang lost his mobile phone,but he was afraid to tell to his parents."​


Ubahlah ke kalimat interrogative dan negative :

"Bambang lost his mobile phone,but he was afraid to tell to his parents."​

Jawab:

(-) Bambang [did not lose] his mobile phone, but he [was not afraid] to tell to his parents.

(?) [did Bambang lose] his mobile phone, but [was he afraid] to tell to his parents.?

Berdasarkan Rumus Past Tense

* Verbal Sentence

(-) S + did not + verb 1 + O/C

= Bambang did not lose his mobile phone,

(?) did + S + verb 1 + O/C?

= did Bambang lose his mobile phone

* Nominal Sentence

(-) S + was/were + not + O/C

= but he was not afraid to tell to his parents.

(?) was/were + S + O/C?

= but was he afraid to tell to his parents.?

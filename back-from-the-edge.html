18. He will graduated from the university when you come back to indonesia .pke interrogrative


Future perfect tense dipakai untuk menyatakan peristiwa yang sudah terjadi dan akan selesai di waktu tertentu di masa depan.

Formulasi future perfect tense:

S + will + have + past particple (verb 3)

Contoh:

(+) I will have finished my homework at 10 p.m.

Arti: Saya akan sudah menyelesaikan PR saya jam 10 malam.

(-) I won't (will not) have finished my homework at 10 p.m.

Arti: Saya tidak akan sudah menyelesaikan PR saya jam 10 malam.

(?) Will I have finished my homework at 10 p.m?

Arti: Akankah saya sudah menyelesaikan PR saya jam 10 malam?


Keterangan waktu yang biasa dipakai untuk tense ini : by tomorrow, at 7 o'clock, by next month, by next year, by the the end of this month, by the end of this year, dll.


Pembahasan

(+) He will be graduated from the university when you come back to Indonesia.

(-) He won't be graduated from the university when you come back to Indonesia.

(?) Will he be graduated from the university when you come back to Indonesia.

31. What happened to Vasco da Gama sailors on the Journey back from India?​


indonesia

Paulo da Gama meninggal di Azores dalam perjalanan pulang, tetapi ketika Vasco da Gama kembali ke Portugal pada September 1499, ia mendapatkan hadiah yang sangat besar sebagai orang yang berhasil mewujudkan rencana yang telah disusun selama 80 tahun. Ia mendapatkan gelar "Admiral Samudera Hindia", dan hak-hak feodal atas Sines dikukuhkan. Ia juga dianugerahi gelar Dom (count) oleh Manuel I.

Pelayaran da Gama membuktikan bahwa pantai Afrika yang lebih jauh (pantai Timur), Contra Costa, adalah penting bagi kepentingan Portugis. Pelabuhan-pelabuhannya menyediakan air bersih dan perbekalan, kayu dan pelabuhan untuk reparasi, dan tempat untuk menunggu sementara musim tidak menguntungkan. Selain itu, komoditas rempah-rempah terbukti juga merupakan kontribusi penting bagi ekonomi Portugal.

inggris

Paulo da Gama died in the Azores on his way home, but when Vasco da Gama returned to Portugal in September 1499, he was rewarded with a huge prize for carrying out a plan that had been drawn up for 80 years. He received the title "Admiral of the Indian Ocean", and feudal rights to Sines were confirmed. He was also awarded the title Dom (count) by Manuel I.

Da Gama's voyage proved that the more distant African coast (East coast), Contra Costa, was of great importance to Portuguese interests. Its ports provided clean water and supplies, timber and a port for repairs, and a place to wait while the season was unfavorable. In addition, the spice commodity proved to be an important contribution to the Portuguese economy.

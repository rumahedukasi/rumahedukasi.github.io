1) United States :
    a handshake, a smile, say a "hello"

2) The British :
    say a "hello"(with friends), a handshake, a social kiss (often just a peck on the cheek)

3) French nationals :
    a handshake, a social kiss (often on both cheeks)

4) Japan :
    a bow (as opposed to a casual handshake and hug)

5) Arab countries :
    a social kiss (often on both cheeks), a handshake (with right hands only and same sex only)

6) Hungarians :
    a social kiss (on both cheeks, from your right to your left), a handshake

7) Belgium :
    a social kiss (on one cheek, regardless of their gender or their relationship)

8) Chinese :
    a nod on their heads with a smile, shake hands

9) Russia :
    a handshake

10) Albania :
    a handshake, a social kiss (on each cheek, depending on how well they know each other), 

11) Armenia :
    a handshake, a social kiss, a light hug1. United State: Man do a handshake, it's unusual if man kiss for greeting. A smile and say a "Hello"
2. The British: Handshake for people meet each other for the first time and social kissing for men and woman who know each other well
3. French Nationals: Handshake and kiss on the both cheeks. Both upon meeting and leaving.
4. Japan: Bow the hand for greet someone, casual handshake, and hug.
5. Arab Countries: Close male is handshake and kiss both cheeks. Handshake with right hand only. Do not offer handshake with opposite gender.
6. Hungarians: Friendly greeting form of kissing each other on the cheeks. Kiss from your right to your left. The casual norm is a firm handshake.
7. Belgium: Kiss on one cheek, regardless of the gender or how well they know each other.
8. Chinese: For meeting for the first time, nod their head and smile, or handshake if in formal situation.
9. Russia: Handshake, kiss on cheek for man to women even kiss hand the women.
10 Albania: Man do handshake, depending on close they are, kiss on both cheek will do. Men to women handshake, kissing on each cheek. Woman to woman, handshake and kiss both cheek.
11. Armenia: A woman need to wait for the man offer his hand for the handshake. Good friend and family, kiss on the each cheek and light hug.

1. integral cos (5x) per [tex]integral \: cos \times \frac{5x}{4} dx[/tex]


KALKULUS

Gunakan sifat integral
[tex] \int a \times f(x) \: dx = a \int f(x) \: dx [/tex]

[tex] \int cos (x) \: dx = sin (x) [/tex]

[tex] dx = 1 / t' dt [/tex]

Jadi, penyelesaiannya yaitu
[tex] \int cos (\frac {5x}{4}) dx \\ \int cos (\frac {5x}{4}) \times \frac{1}{t'} dt \\ \int cos (\frac {5x}{4}) \times \frac {4}{5} dt \\ \int \frac {4 cos (t)}{5} dt \\ \frac {4}{5} \int \cos (t) dt \\ \frac {4 \sin {\frac {5}{4}}}{5} [/tex]

40. integral cos³ 5x dx​


Jawab:

[tex]\int \cos^{3}\left(5x\right)dx=\frac{1}{15}\sin\left(5x\right)\left(\cos^{2}\left(5x\right)+2\right)+C[/tex]

¹⁄₁₅ sin(5x)( cos²(5x)+2 ) + C

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Gunakan metode substitusi

[tex]\int \cos^{3}(5x)dx[/tex]

Substitusi

[tex]u=5x\\dx=\frac{du}{5}[/tex]

Menjadi

[tex]\frac{1}{5}\int \cos^{3}\left(u\right)du[/tex]

Gunakan rumus reduksi integral trigonometri dimana

[tex]\int \cos^{n}\left(x\right)dx = \frac{1}{n}\cos^{n-1}\left(x\right)\sin\left(x\right)+\frac{n-1}{n}\int\cos^{n-2}\left(x\right)dx[/tex]

[tex]\frac{1}{5}\int \cos^{3}\left(u\right)du\\=\frac{1}{5}\left(\frac{1}{3}\cos^{2}\left(u\right)\sin\left(u\right)+\frac{2}{3}\int\cos\left(u\right)du\right)\\=\frac{1}{15}\cos^{2}\left(u\right)\sin\left(u\right)+\frac{2}{15}\int\cos\left(u\right)du[/tex]

Ingat bahwa [tex]\int \cos\left(x\right)dx = \sin\left(x\right)+C[/tex]

[tex]=\frac{1}{15}\cos^{2}\left(u\right)\sin\left(u\right)+\frac{2}{15}\left(\sin\left(u\right)+C\right)\\=\frac{1}{15}\cos^{2}\left(u\right)\sin\left(u\right)+\frac{2}{15}\sin\left(u\right)+C[/tex]

Substitusi kembali nilai [tex]u=5x[/tex]

[tex]=\frac{1}{15}\cos^{2}\left(5x\right)\sin\left(5x\right)+\frac{2}{15}\sin\left(5x\right)+C\\=\frac{1}{15}\sin\left(5x\right)\left(\cos^{2}\left(5x\right)+2\right)+C[/tex]

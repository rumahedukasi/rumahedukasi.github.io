8. Gunakan penyusun kalimat PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE example: (+)The car is mine (?)Is the car mine? The car is not mine. 1.I (be) a student (+) (?) (-) 2.She (be) my friend 3.My friend (be) in the class 4.I and my Mother (be) on the way 2,3,4 jawabannya dibikin seperti no.1


Jawaban:

1. i (be) a student

  (+) i am a student

  (-) i am not a student

  (?) am i a student?

2. she (be) my friend

  (+) she is my friend

  (-) she isn't my friend

  (?) is she my friend?

3. my friend (be) in the class

   (+) my friend is in the class

   (-) my friend isn't in the class

   (?) is my friend in the class?

4. i and my mother (be) on the way

   (+) i and my mother are on the way

   (-) i and my mother aren't on the way

   (?) are i and my mother on the way?

Penjelasan:

present countinuous tense:

(+) Subject + to be + verb + ing + object/adverb/adjective

example: i am washing car

(-) Subject + to be + not + verb + ing + object/adverb/adjective

example: i am not washing car

(?) to be + subject + verb + ing + obj/adj/adv + ?

example: am i washing car?

to be: I (am), She/he/it (is), they/we (are)

Pelajaran : bahasa inggris

Materi      : present continuous

37. Jasper and jose are diving on different sea depth jose is on 10 m below the sea surface while jasper is 5 in below him. if the density of the seawater is 1100 kg/m^3. find the amount of pressure exerted on jose.​


Penjelasan:

h Jose = 10m

h Jasper = 5m

ρ Air Laut = 1100 kg/m^3

g = 10 m/s^2

P Jose = ?

P Jose = ρ . g . h Jose

= 1100 . 10 . 10

= 110000 Pascal ⇒ 110 kiloPascal

Note: Kalau gravitasi bukan 10, bisa diganti "g"-nya

40. I went to see a friend of mine. jika kata ini di ubah menjadi negatif maka bagaimana perubahannya


--•Simple Past Tense•--
Responsio :
Pada dasarnya, cara mengubah kalimat positive dalam simple past tense hanya dengan menambah kata did not atau didn't setelah subject (apapun subjectnya) dan mengubah verb nya dari verb 2 menjadi verb 1 atau verb base.

Dalam kalimat ini verb went menjadi didn't go

(+) I went to see a friend of mine
(-) I didn't go to see a friend of mine

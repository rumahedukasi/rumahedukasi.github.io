Degrees of comparison merupakan tingkat perbandingan yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu tingkat perbandingan pada kata sifat pada kata benda tertentu. Berdasarkan gambar, compare my quote and the quote in the caption are:

Quote 1: "The clearest way into the Universe is through a forest wilderness." The answer is: "The easiest way to see the universe is through the wilderness because in the forest it is beautiful and there are many things that are pure from nature."

Quote 2: "I thank you God for this most amazing day, for the leaping greenly spirits of trees, and for the blue dream of sky and for everything which is natural, which is infinite, which is yes." The answer is: "About how to be grateful to God for having been given a perfect day, complete with natural and limitless natural beauty."

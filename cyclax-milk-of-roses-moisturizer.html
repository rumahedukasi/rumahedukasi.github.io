8. Cita has two bottles of milk. The ratio of the volume of milk in Bottle A to the volume of milk in Bottle B is 3:4. The volume of milk in Bottle A is 120 ml. Find the total volume of milk in both bottles. Pake caranya yah dan jika kalian tidak ngerti silahkan kalian translate ( terjemahkan)


Cita memiliki 2 botol susu

Rasio volume A : volume B = 3 : 4
Jika,
volume A = 120ml

Maka, jika kita ingin mencari volume total adalah dengan cara,

Va / Vb = Va' / Vb'

3 : 4 = 120 : Vb'

kalikan LHS dengan 1 namun dalam bentuk x / x

3x : 4x = 120 : Vb'

Secara matematis, bisa langsung kita ambil kesimpulan bahwa,

3x = 120
x = 40

Vb' = 4x
Vb' = 4 ( 40 ) = 160

Maka Vt atau Vtotal = Va' + Vb' = 120 + 160 = 280 ml

17. 1 Lisa bought some roses. She used of the roses to decorate the living 2 - 1 room. She then placed of the remaining roses in her sister's room. She also 3 — dropped some roses into 3 vases, each with 4 roses. Lastly, she had 24 roses left. How many roses did she buy at first?​


Jawab:

﻿60 roses

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Let's solve this problem step by step.

Let's assume that Lisa bought x roses initially.

According to the information given:

- Lisa used 2/5 of the roses to decorate the living room, which is (2/5) * x.

- She then placed 1/3 of the remaining roses in her sister's room, which is (1/3) * (3/5) * x.

- She also dropped some roses into 3 vases, each with 4 roses. So the number of roses left after placing some in the vases is (3/5) * x - 3 * 4.

- Lastly, Lisa had 24 roses left, which is (3/5) * x - 12 = 24.

Now, we can solve the equation:

(3/5) * x - 12 = 24

Add 12 to both sides of the equation:

(3/5) * x = 36

Multiply both sides by 5/3 to isolate x:

x = 36 * (5/3)

x = 60

Therefore, Lisa bought 60 roses initially.

37. 1 Lisa bought some roses. She used of the roses to decorate the living 2 - 1 room. She then placed of the remaining roses in her sister's room. She also 3 - dropped some roses into 3 vases, each with 4 roses. Lastly, she had 24 roses left. How many roses did she buy at first?​


Jawaban:

Let's solve the problem step by step:

Let's assume the number of roses Lisa bought initially is "x."

1. Lisa used 1/2 of the roses to decorate the living room. This means she used (1/2)x roses for decoration.

The number of roses remaining after decorating the living room is (x - (1/2)x) = (1/2)x.

2. Lisa then placed 1/3 of the remaining roses in her sister's room. This means she placed (1/3) * (1/2)x = (1/6)x roses in her sister's room.

The number of roses remaining after placing roses in her sister's room is ((1/2)x - (1/6)x) = (1/3)x.

3. Lisa also dropped some roses into 3 vases, each with 4 roses. So, the number of roses dropped into the vases is 3 * 4 = 12.

The number of roses remaining after putting roses in the vases is (1/3)x - 12.

4. Lastly, Lisa had 24 roses left. This means the remaining roses are equal to 24.

(1/3)x - 12 = 24.

Now, let's solve the equation to find the value of x:

(1/3)x - 12 = 24

(1/3)x = 24 + 12

(1/3)x = 36

x = 36 * 3

x = 108

Therefore, Lisa bought 108 roses at first.

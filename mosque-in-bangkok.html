4. how old is the mosque? well it...in 1870


how old is the mosque? well it (built) in 1870
artinya: berapa umur mesjid ini? ini (dibangun) pada 1870.
terima kasih ♡

Answer:


The sentence for the answer above is ideally answered by using passive form as it focuses on the object or in this case 'it', which refers to 'the mosque'. So, the proper answer is as follows

'How old is the mosque?' 'Well, it was built/ erected in 1870.' (the past passive form is used as there is the definite time signal of the past time 'in 1870'. The pattern is O + was/were + V1 (by S) + complement)


Kelas: SMP

Mapel: English

Kategori: passive form

8. write 3 notices in the mosque​


Here are 3 announcements that can be found in a mosque:

Prayer Schedule: This announcement will state the daily prayer schedule, including the times for Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha prayers.

Islamic Study: This announcement can state the schedule and information about Islamic studies to be held in the mosque, including the topics and speakers who will give the lecture.

Ramadan Program: This announcement will state information about special programs to be held during the month of Ramadan, such as Tarawih, Iftar, and others. It may also include the daily schedule of activities during the month of Ramadan.

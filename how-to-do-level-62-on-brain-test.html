11. how to say hope "you will have a mathematics test"​


Expression of hope

Situation: You will have a mathematics test

Ungkapan 1

Pattern: I + hope + subjek + can/will + verb1

I hope I can pass the matemathics test wellI hope I will pass the matemathics test well

Ungkapan 2

Pattern: I + hope + that + subjek + will, can + verb1

I hope that I can do the matemathics test well tomorrowI hope that I will do the matemathics test well tomorrow

Ungkapan 3

Pattern: I + hope + to + verb1

I hope to pass the matemathics test well tomorrow

15. Help me everyone;) The assignment: Which one do you prefer "I can't afford it" or "How can I afford it"? Why? Please relate your answer to how our brain works.


Jawaban:

I'll prefer "How can I afford it?"

Penjelasan:

Why? because if you choose "I can't afford it", you have failed mentally and that negative energy will make your brain telling you to stop trying because it makes you think that it is impossible to afford something that is actually possible if you have the right mindset.

If you choose "How can I afford it?", that means you are willing to try and your brain will start thinking on how to afford that thing, that makes you either telling yourself "It is affordable!" or back to "I can't afford it". But still, I'd rather choose this one over the negative one because you never know what you are capable of.

27. 62. The respond of 'How do you do' is .... How are you doing? How is life? a. b. c. d. How do you do? How are things?​


Jawaban:

Greetings:

D. How do you do?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal pilihan D. How do you do? dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Greetings (salam) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Salam How do you do? diucapkan ketika bertemu dengan seseorang yang belum dikenal untuk membuka percakapan dengan orang tersebut.

Ketika seseorang yang belum dikenal mengucapkan how do you do? maka jawabannya juga How do you do? jadi jawabannya adalah pilihan D. How do you do?

Semoga membantu ya.

9. Manakah Kalimat Simple Present Tense yang benar? a. He feeds, and plays with his cat every morningb. He feeds,and play with his cat every morning


ANSWER
________

=> Simple present
a. He feeds, and plays with his cat every morning

Reason;
--------------
because "HE" is a Single third person, then we must add the S-suffix to its verb.

(karena"HE"adalah orang ketiga Tunggal,maka kita harus menambahkan akhiran-S pada kata kerjanya)

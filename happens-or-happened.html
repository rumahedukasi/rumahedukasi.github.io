2. beda nya happend, happen, happened, sama happens apa sih? pusing nih bantu ya..


Kelas : SMA
Pelajaran : bahasa Inggris
Kategori : verb
Kata kunci : happen, present verb, past verb, past participle verb

Pembahasan:
Happen (verb), yang membedakannya:
1. Happen dan happens digunakan untuk present tense dan future tense. Yang membedakan adalah subject-nya.
I/you/they/we + happen
She/he/it/name + happens
I/you/they/we/she/he/it + will + happen
I/you/they/we/she/he/it + am/is/are+was/were + going to + happen
Contoh:
It happens regularly.
Flood will happen if we throw rubbish everywhere.

2. Happened, digunakan untuk past tense dan past participle tense.
Contoh:
(past tense - v2) It happened last year.
(past participle - v3) That thing would have happened if I hadnot studied hard.

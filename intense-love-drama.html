4. Mendesis

→ sizzling adalah adjective dari sizzle yang berarti suatu desisan terhadap suatu benda

→ spark adalah sesuatu menunjukkan pencerahan (penerangan) menyebabkan sesuatu tersebut dapat mengeluarkan kilauan

→ merupakan rasa mental yang dimiliki seseorang apabila sudah bekerja payah dalam beraktivitas yang menyebabkan kecapean

7. Kegaringan

→ crisp adalah sesuatu yang ditunjuk dinyatakan memiliki tekstur (bentuk) yang mudah retak dan garing

8. Menegangkan

→ menunjukkan suatu suasana intensif atau membawakan rasa seseorang terhadap sesuatu yang cukup membuat dirinya kewalahan atas menghadapi hal itu

Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

Global warming, also known as climate change, is a pressing issue that has been affecting the Earth's climate and environment in numerous ways. One of the major consequences of global warming is the occurrence of longer periods of drought in various regions around the world. As the temperature rises, evaporation rates increase, leading to reduced moisture content in the soil and decreased water availability. This prolonged dry spell can have severe impacts on agriculture, water resources, and ecosystems, affecting both human livelihoods and biodiversity.

Another devastating effect of global warming is the intensification of tropical storms. Warmer ocean temperatures provide more energy to fuel these storms, leading to stronger and more frequent hurricanes, typhoons, and cyclones. Such extreme weather events cause widespread destruction, displacing communities, and leaving behind a trail of devastation.

The melting of glaciers and ice sheets is yet another consequence of global warming. As temperatures rise, ice at the polar regions and high-altitude mountains begin to melt, contributing to rising sea levels. This phenomenon poses a significant threat to coastal communities and low-lying islands, leading to the loss of land and increased risks of flooding and coastal erosion.

Prolonged heatwaves have become more common due to global warming. Rising temperatures result in prolonged periods of extreme heat, putting human health at risk and straining energy resources. Heatwaves can exacerbate existing health conditions, lead to heat-related illnesses, and increase energy demands for cooling, further intensifying the impact of global warming.

Global warming is also associated with an increase in extreme rainfall events and wildfires. As the atmosphere warms, it can hold more moisture, leading to more intense rainfall in certain regions, causing flash floods and landslides. Additionally, the prolonged periods of heat and dryness create favorable conditions for wildfires to spread rapidly, causing vast forest and bushland destruction, and posing risks to communities and wildlife.

Addressing global warming is of utmost importance to mitigate these impacts and safeguard the planet's future. International efforts to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and transition to renewable energy sources are essential to combat global warming and prevent further climate-related disasters. Sustainable land use practices, conservation efforts, and community resilience-building are also vital to adapting to the changing climate and protecting vulnerable populations and ecosystems from the effects of global warming. It is a collective responsibility to take action to combat global warming and ensure a sustainable and habitable planet for future generations.

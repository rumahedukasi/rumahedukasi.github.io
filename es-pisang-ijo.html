16. bagaimana cara membuat minuman es Buto ijo​


Penjelasan:

alat dan bahan:

*Sirup kental rasa melon sesuaikan

*1 buah Daging kelapa muda (kerok dengan bentuk memanjang)

*150 gram Kacang hijau (rebus dahulu hingga matang)

*150 gram Nata de coco (Yang masih segar kemudian cuci hingga bersih)

*150 gram kolang-kaling (Iris tipis – tipis)

*½ kaleng susu kental putih

*4 sendok makan sagu mutiara (Rebus dahulu)

*2 sendok makan biji selasih (Rendam dahulu)

*Es batu secukupnya (geprek dahulu lalu hancurkan halus)

Langkah untuk membuat Es Buto Ijo :

Langkah pertama untuk cara membuat es buto ijo silahkan anda ambil sebuah wadah yang berukuran cukup besar, kemudian masukkan kolang- kaling, nata de coco, sagu mutiara, biji selasih serta kacang hijau sambil diaduk-aduk hingga merata.

Cara membuat es buto ijo selanjutnya masukkan daging kelapa yang telah di kerok sambil diaduk kembali hingga tercampur rata.

Langkah berikutnya silahkan Anda tambahkan es batu yang telah dihancurkan serta beri sirup kental di atasnya.

Selesai kini Anda berhasil membuatnya dan es buto ijo siap untuk disajikan.


17. harga satu bungkus pisang goreng ijo rp15. 000.00 sedangkan harga s3bungkus pisang goreng keju rp20. 000.00 tentukan perbandingan harga antara pisang goreng ijo dan pisang goreng keju​


Jawaban

Perbandingannya adalah 3 : 4.

Penyelesaian Soal

Diketahui:

- Harga satu bungkus Pisang Goreng Ijo = Rp15.000,00.

- Harga satu bungkus Pisang Goreng Keju = Rp20.000,00.

Maka, perbandingannya adalah,

= Pisang Goreng Ijo : Pisang Goreng Keju

= 15.000 : 20.000

= (15.000 ÷ 5.000) : (20.000 ÷ 5.000)

= 3 : 4

19. persentasi bahasa inggris..cara membuat es pisang ijo​


Leather Material:

1. 100 grams of flour

2. 100 ml of water

3. 1 tsp margarine

4. Salt to taste

5. Pandan paste to taste

6. 3 medium size plantains

Ingredients vla or marrow porridge:

1. 200 ml coconut milk (coconut milk 65 ml + water)

2. 25 grams of granulated sugar

3. 3 tsp flour

4. Salt to taste

5. Vanilla is sufficiently liquid

Other Ingredients:

1. Ice cubes or shaved ice to taste

2. DHT syrup or cocopandan syrup to taste

How to make:

1. To make the skin, first prepare the pan then boil water, margarine, salt and pandan paste. Boil until boil then reduce heat, add flour while stirring quickly. Turn off the heat.

2. After the mixture has cooled a bit, knead the mixture until everything is combined. If the color is not green enough to add pandan paste.

3. Peel the banana then wrap it with the skin mixture.

4. Heat the steam, wrap the lid with a clean napkin. Spread with oil in a banana container so that the banana is not sticky. Then steam for 10-15 minutes. Remove and cut according to taste.

5. To make the vein, first boil coconut milk, sugar, salt and liquid vanilla. Stir with the whisker until it boils. Then dissolve the flour with water. When the coconut milk boils, add the flour solution while stirring constantly with the whisker until it thickens and boils. Turn off the heat.

6. Prepare a bowl or plate. Put bananas, ice cubes or shaved ice and vla.

7. Pour the syrup and green banana ice is ready to be served. Good luck!

Pembahasan

A. untung

Penjelasan:

hitung omzet dulu :

= 500 x 8.000

= 4.000.000

hitung biaya operasional :

= 1.200.000 + 2.500.000

= 3.700.000

untung/rugi = omzet - biaya operasional

= 4.000.000 - 3.700.000

= 300.000

sehingga, usaha dulkemit dapat disimpulkan untung karena omzetnya melebihi biaya operasional.

39. langkah langkah membuat es pisang ijo dalam bahasa inggris


Ingredients green banana ice (es pisang ijo) - 50 grams of Sago Flour - 175 grams of corn flour, sieve it - 1/2 teaspoon of salt - 5 pieces of Suji Leaves - 6 Banana Fruit - 300 ml of boiled water - Shredded Chedar Cheese - Chocolate Meses - Strawberry Syrup - Sweetened condensed milk - Shreded Ice how to make green banana ice ( es pisang ijo ) : 1. Mix all of cornflour, suji leaves, salt, and boiled water. Heat it all up with low fire level, and stir it until be a dough. and then put the sagu flour into the dough until be a perfect dough 2. Divide the dough to be 6 part, and then trimmed it until be 1/2 cm of thick 3. Cover the banana with these dough, after that boil these all banana until float, float is the alert that all banana with dough has cooked perfectly. 4. Put in a big bowl, with Shredded ice, Sweetened condensed milk, Strawberry Syrup, Chredded Chedar Cheese, and Chocolate meses.

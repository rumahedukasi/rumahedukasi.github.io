31. people say that I am....than my older brother.a. more intelligenterb. more intelligentc. intelligenterd. most intelligent​


B. More intelligent

PEMBAHASANComparative Degree

Comparative Degree bermakna lebih dan biasanya selalu ada kata than (daripada)

1 syllable: adjective + er (bigger)2-3 syllable: more + adjective (more intelligent)Jika berakhiran "y": adjective + ier (friendlier)

Keterangan:

People say that I'm possitive that I am .... than my little brother

a.more intelligenter = salah penulisanb.more intelligent = comparative degreec.intelligenter = bukan comparisond.most intelligent = Superlative degree

KESIMPULANkarena ada kata Than (daripada) maka gunakan comparative degree, more + adjective = more intelligent.

Jadi, Jawaban adalah D.

=====Semoga Membantu=====

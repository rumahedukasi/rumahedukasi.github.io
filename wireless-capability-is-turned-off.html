12. 18. The formula of expression of capability is​


Penjelasan:

Ability adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan bentuk kemampuan. Kata yang paling umum digunakan adalah can, could (past) dan managed to. Berikut ini adalah formula untuk menyatakan ability dalam bentuk present tense:

1. Is/am/are able to + main verb

Thomas is able to play the piano.

Katy is not able to play the piano.

2. Can + main verb

Katy can play the flute.

Thomas can't play the flute.

Willingness adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan bentuk kesediaan. Kata yang paling umum digunakan adalah will, would (past). Berikut ini adalah contoh kalimat untuk menyatakan willingness dalam bentuk present tense:

We’ll see you tomorrow.

Perhaps dad will led me the car.

Contoh dialog:

Katy: Can you help me with my piano practice tomorrow?

Thomas: I'm afraid I can't make it for Sunday. What about next week on Saturday. I will be free and then I can help you whole day.

Katy: Sure, that will be great! Thanks Thomas.

21. what is expression of capability​


Jawaban:

English

Expressing capability / incapability. - Although my father is now 75 years old, he still has the capability of driving car well. - He is still capable of driving car well. - The situation is capable of improvement. - They think I have got the qualifications needed for the job.

Indo

Mengekspresikan kemampuan / ketidakmampuan. - Meskipun ayah saya sekarang sudah berumur 75 tahun, dia tetap memiliki kemampuan mengendarai mobil dengan baik. - Dia masih bisa mengendarai mobil dengan baik. - Situasi bisa diperbaiki. - Mereka pikir saya punya kualifikasi yang dibutuhkan untuk pekerjaan itu.

27. they had turned off the lamp rubahlah kebentuk possitive


SPEAKING

That is possitive sentence. Using past perfect, the formula is
• (+) S + had + past participle (possitive form)
• (-) S + had not + past participle (negative form)
• (?) Had + S + past participle (question form / interrogative form)

So,
1 ) the negative form is
- They hadn't turned of the lamp
2 ) The interrogative form is
- Had they turned of the lamp?

18. Buatlah - past continue (5) - past tense (5) - present tense (5) - past tense + while + past continue - past continue + when + past tense - while * past continue + past tense - when + past tende + past continue


PAST TENSE VS PAST CONTINOUS  Jawaban    Pendahuluan  

Simple past tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung di waktu lampau. Keterangan waktu yang menunjukan waktu lampau dibutuhkan disini, seperti: yesterday, last week, two days ago, two hours ago, last night, dan lain lain.

Rumus simple past adalah:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O?

Past Continuous Tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung dan mempunyai proses di masa lampau. Disini penggunaan kata when dan while sering digunakan ketika past continuous tense bertembu simple past tense.  

Rumus Past continuous adalah:

(+) S + was/were + Ving + O

(-) S + was/were not + Ving + O

(?) Was/were + S + Ving + O?

was = I, He, She, It

were = You, They, We

Pembahasan  

 Buatlah kalimat:

Past Continuous:

I was watching TV at the same time yesterday.Mrs. Susan was teaching us yesterday at 9 am.They were playing football here yesterday at 3 pmWe were visiting Tina yesterday afternoon.My father was working at the same time yesterday.

Simple Past Tense

I bought a new car last monthWe won the game yesterday.They were the champion of Indonesian League last season.Rina visited her grandmother last week.He was the victim of earthquake in Palu.

Simple Present Tense

I always go to school at 6.30 amThe sun rises from the east.Where do you come from?I don't know what you mean.She plays piano beautifully.

Past Continuous + while + Past Continuous

My father was washing his car while my mother was sweeping the floor.

Past Continuous + when + past tense

I was playing video game when you came.

While + Past Continuous + Past Tense

While I was daydreaming in the class, Mrs. Susan called my name.

When + Past Tense + Past Continuous

When my mother knocked the door, I was sleeping.

27. buatlah kalimat present tense, past, future, Dan present continuous Dari kata berikut!1. BUY present tense= ?past= ?future= ?present continuous= ?2. TAKEpresent tense= ?past= ?future= ?present continuous= ?3. GETpresent tense= ?past= ?future= ?present continuous= ?JANGAN NGASAL!​


ANSWER

1. BUY

present tense= i buy a book

past= I was buy a book

future= I will buy a book

present continuous= I am buying a book now.

2. TAKE

present tense= She takes a bus to school

past= She took a bus to school

future= She will take a bus to school

present continuous= She is taking a bus to school

3. GET

present tense= I get a fever now.

past= I got a fever

future= I will get a fever

present continuous= I am getting a fever

EXPLANATION

Rumus SIMPLE Present Tense :

(+) S + Verb -s/es + O

Rumus Simple Past Tense :

(+) S + Verb2 + O atau,(+) S + was/were + Complement

Rumus Simple Future Tense :

(+) S + will/shall + Verb1

Rumus Present Continuous Tense :

(+) S + to be (is/am/are) + Verb-ing + Complement

past= Dia naik bus ke sekolah

future= Dia akan naik bus ke sekolah

3. GET

present tense = aku demam sekarang

past = aku demam (lampau)

future = aku akan demam

present continuous= saya sedang demam

34. "i have cleaned up my room" ← apakah kalimat ini adalah kalimat "past tense"?kalo tidak ,coba berikan 1 contoh kalimat past tense​


"I have cleaned up my room"  bukan kalimat past tense, tetapi present perfect tense.

Contoh kalimat past tense: I cleaned up my room this morning.

Pembahasan

Kalimat "I have cleaned  up my room" berpola subject + have + verb 3 + object. Ini merupakan pola present perfect tense.

Sedangkan simple past tense berpola subject + verb2.

Contoh simple past tense lainnya adalah I wrote this letter last night

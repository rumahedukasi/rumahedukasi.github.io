8. (+) the boys will build the tentthe negative sentence (-) is . .a. the boys build the tentb. the boys won't build the tent c. will the boys build the tent d. the boys want build the tent​


~ Answer:b. the boys won't build the tent.

.

~ Discussion:

Halo, sobat cerdas! Agar kita dapat merubah pernyataan positif (+) tersebut, kita perlu memerhatikan tense-nya dulu.

» The boys will build the tent.kalimat di atas termasuk kalimat verbal dengan auxiliary: will menunjukkan suatu pekerjaan akan terjadi nanti di masa depan menggunakan bentuk simple futureS + will + V1 + ...

Kalimat negatif (-) menyatakan hal atau pekerjaan yang bertentangan dengan kalimat positif dengan dibuktikan adanya not setelah helping verb.

S + will not + V1 + ...

Will not dapat menggunakan contraction dengan merubahnya menjadi won't.

JAWABANNYA:

b. the boys won't build the tent.

[tex]m_p[/tex] = Samuel's previous mass

37. 13. What should I do to build self confidence ? saran​


Jawaban:1. Stop comparing yourself to others First, instill in yourself, that life is not a competition. You may often feel inferior to your colleagues' achievements, envy when you hear that your friend's salary is higher, or feel insecure because you think other people are physically better.

2. Love yourself with a healthy lifestyle and self-care An unhealthy lifestyle can make your body tormented. Call it, not getting enough rest, lack of nutritious food, or lazy to exercise. In fact, a healthy lifestyle not only keeps you physically fit, but also improves your mental state and self-confidence. In a study published in Neuropsychiatric Disease and Treatment, regular physical activity has been shown to present a positive body image. A positive body image helps you to be more confident. Combine a healthy lifestyle, which is accompanied by self-care. Going through this step means that you are taking time to please yourself, in a positive way. 3. Accept self-doubt Having self-doubt is natural, even for experienced people. You don't have to be 100% confident before you do something. A research published in Psychology of Sport and Exercise proves that self-doubt that arises, actually helps you give a good performanceAccepting doubt does not mean doing an activity without preparation. You still have to understand what you're going through, while still embracing your own self-doubt. Careful preparation and understanding can increase self-confidence.

4. Practice self-compassion The term self-compassion refers to understanding yourself, when you are hit by an unpleasant moment. For example, when you are overcome with failure or make mistakes, which often lowers your self-confidence. By practicing self-compassion, understand that you are a complete human being. Experiencing failure and making mistakes is very human. There's nothing wrong with talking to yourself or laughing at yourself, when experiencing an unpleasant incident.

5. Fight negative thoughts When you want to lift weights at the gym, negative thoughts may whisper that you can't lift those weights. The best way to respond to negative thoughts is to fight them. You can still do things that your brain doesn't think you can do. Not a few people who can hide feelings of inferiority. Although it is common, sometimes you need help from a counselor or psychologist, if your self-confidence does not increase. If low self-esteem has interfered with daily activities, including work, social relationships, personal relationships, and education, it is recommended that you seek professional help. Because, low self-esteem can be rooted in more severe causes, such as traumatic experiences in the past

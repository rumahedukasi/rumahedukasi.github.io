11. Bentuk negatif dari kalimat I took care of my mother that was sick Last night


Bentuk negatif dari kalimat I took care of my mother that was sick Last night adalah I did not take care of my mother that was sick Last night.

Pembahasan

Kalimat soal dalam bentuk Positive yang di dalamnya terdapat:

verb 2 “took” keterangan waktu “last night”

Maka hal ini menunjukkan bahwa kalimat tersebut adalah Simple Past.

Simple Past adalah tenses yg digunakan utk menyatakan Kegiatan yg terjadi di masa lalu atau lampau.

➡️ Keterangan waktunya:

YesterdayLast night ✔️Two days agoA few minutes ago

➡️ Rumusnya:

(+) S + Verb 2 + O/C(-) S + did not + Verb 1 + O/C(?) Did + S + Verb 1 + O/C

Kesimpulan

Bentuk Positive

(+) I took care of my mother that was sick Last night

Bentuk Negative

(-) I did not take care of my mother that was sick Last night ✔️

Bentuk Interrogative

(?) Did I take care of my mother that was sick Last night?

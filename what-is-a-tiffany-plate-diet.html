25. mika (eat) a plate of rice bowl. what is the correct word in brackets?


Jawaban:

The correct word in brackets is "ate."

The sentence should be: "Mika ate a plate of rice bowl."

Penjelasan:

The word "ate" is the past tense form of the verb "eat." In the sentence, it indicates that Mika consumed a plate of rice bowl in the past. Past tense is used to describe actions that have already happened.

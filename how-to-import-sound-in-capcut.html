Jawaban:

The history of sound recording - which has progressed in waves, driven by the invention and commercial introduction of new technologies — can be roughly divided into four main periods:

the "Acoustic" era, 1877 to 1925

the "Electrical" era, 1925 to 1945

the "Magnetic" era, 1945 to 1975

the "Digital" era, 1975 to the present day.

Thomas Edison , photographed by Mathew Brady in Washington, April 1878

Experiments of capturing sound on a recording medium for preservation and reproduction began in earnest during the Industrial Revolution of the 1800s. Many pioneering attempts to record and reproduce sound were made during the latter half of the 19th century – notably Scott's phonautograph of 1857 – and these efforts culminated in the invention of the phonograph by Thomas Edison in 1877. Digital recording emerged in the late 20th century and has since flourished with the popularity of digital music and online streaming services.

Ending sound in phonics:

• Ending Sound -ay. Ini adalah salah satu suara yang perlu dikenali – anak kecil dapat melatih suara 'ay' dengan kata-kata yang familiar ini.

• Mengakhiri Suara -dan. Kata-kata yang familiar untuk membantu anak kecil melatih bunyi 'dan'.

• Sound -ell. Kata-kata yang familiar ini untuk membantu anak kecil melatih suara 'ell'.

• Sound -ee. Hanya satu kata yang mungkin memerlukan bantuan anak kecil dalam lembar kerja ini – dapatkah Anda memilih yang mana? Kata kunci sederhana kami – lebah, lihat, pohon, tiga, lutut.

• Sound -semua. Kata-kata yang familiar ini untuk membantu anak kecil melatih suara 'semua'.

• Diakhiri dengan 'ing'. Kata kunci sederhana kami – gambar fitur ring, sing, wing, kind, swing dapat dikenali dan familiar bagi anak kecil.

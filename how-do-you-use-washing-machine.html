Front loading washing machine:
1. Open the washing machine door.
2. Put the clothes inside the machine.
3. Close the washing machine door.
4. Open the detergent shelf and put detergents, softeners, etc inside the shelf.

Cara menggunakan mesin cuci (washing machine) dalam bahasa Inggris adalah pertama-tama kita harus mengidentifikasi material dan juga steps/method dalam aktivitas ini, sehingga langkah-langkah tersebut dapat diuraikan kemudian menjadi sebuah procedural text.

Berikut adalah pembahasannya.


PembahasanHow to use washing machine

Material:

1. Clothes

2. Detergent powder

3. Washing machine

4. Fabric softener (optional)


Steps/method:

1. Add your dirty clothes and detergent to the drum of the machine.

2. Choose the right washing speed/cycle

3. Set the water temperature, preferably cool.

4. Press the ON button.

5. Take off all the washed clothes from the machine once it’s done.


How to play hide and seek

Material:

1. Players/Children

2. Space for hiding


Steps/method:

1. Choose one player to close his/her eyes for several seconds, usually counting to 100. This player called seeker.

2. Other players hide themselves as soon as possible.

3. Seeker opens his/her eyes and try to find other players.

4. The first player found will be the next seeker, and the last to be found is the winner.

21. The total cost 2 similar televisions and 5 similar washing machines is 7215 dollar . each washing machine cost 216 dollar less than a television. how much do 3 televisions cost?


Total is $7,215 for 2 TV’s & 5 washing machines

each washing machine is $216 less than the TV’s

2 × $216 = $432

$7,215 minus $432 = $6,783 divided by 7 = $969

2 TV’s at $969 + $216 = $1,185 x 2 = Original Cost Total of $2,370 for 2 TV’s

5 Washing machines x $969 = Total of $4,845

$2,370+ $4,845 = Total of $7,215

Answer: The cost of an additional TV would cost another $1,185 to the original 2 TV’s listed above ($2,370 + $1,185) = New total of $3,555 for 3 TV’s

28. what do you have to do first when you want to use a fax machine​


Jawaban:

1. Make sure the fax machine is plugged into a power source and also plugged into a working phone jack.

2. Turn the fax machine on.

Obtain the fax number of the destination fax machine.

3. Gather the documents you want to send and put them in the order you want them to be received.

4. Fill out a separate piece of paper called a coversheet with the recipient's name, fax number/phone number, your name, your phone number, a short message and number of pages (including coversheet).

5. Lay the documents face-up in the fax machine feeder tray with the coversheet on top

6. Dial the recipient's fax number (dialing instructions for international calls)

7. Press the "fax" or "send" button, depending on the particular fax machine model
